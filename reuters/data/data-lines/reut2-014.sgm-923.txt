PHILADELPHIA SEES CURRENCY OPTIONS TRADE IN ASIA

The Philadelphia Stock Exchange will soon open offices in Hong Kong and Tokyo to help boost Far Eastern participation in foreign currency options before it starts evening trading in four or five months time, exchange president Nicholas Giordano told reporters.
 Japanese institutions are already major players in foreign currency options traded in Philadelphia, using subsidiaries or agents to trade during the current daytime session.  REUTER &#3;
