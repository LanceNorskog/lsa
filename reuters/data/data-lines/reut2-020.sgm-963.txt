NYSE TO HOLD PRESS CONFERENCE

The New York Stock Exchange said it will hold a press conference at 16:20 EDT.
 The exchange released no further details.
 The Dow Jones industrial average fell over 500 points in trading on Monday, exceeding the Oct 28, 1929 decline that heralded the Great Depression.  Reuter &#3;
