<AMOUR INC> TO MAKE ACQUISITION

Amour Inc said it has entered into a letter of intent to acquire <Bard International Associates Inc> for 70 mln common shares in a transaction that would give former Bard shareholders control of the combined company.
 Bard makes tennis and squash racquets and accessories.  Reuter &#3;
