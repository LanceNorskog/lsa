HENLEY GROUP <HENG> UNIT WINS NEWS PLANT ORDER

Henley Group Inc unit, Wheelabrator Technologies Inc, said it won a 243 mln dlrs contract from <Mississippi Chemical Corp> unit Newsprint South Inc.
 The company said the contract is for the construction of a 224,000 ton-per-year newsprint production facility in Grenada.  Reuter &#3;
