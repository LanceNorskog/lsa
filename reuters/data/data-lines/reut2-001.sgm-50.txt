UNITED-GUARDIAN <UNIR>, <FEDERAL> SIGN CONTRACT

United-Guardian Inc said it signed a contract with <Federal Health Corp> covering the exclusive marketing of Warexin, a disinfectant for kidney dialysis machines, hospital equipment and instruments.
 Under the contract, United-Guardian said Federal will continue to supply Hospal, a subsidiary of <Sandoz Ltd> and <Rhone Poulenc S.A.> with all its Warexin requirements.  Reuter &#3;
