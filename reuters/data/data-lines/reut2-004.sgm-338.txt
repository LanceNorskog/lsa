MILLIPORE <MILI> ACQUIRES EQUITY IN PROTEIN

<Protein Databases Inc> said Millipore Corp through its venture capital subsidiary, Millicorp, acquired an equity position in the company.
 Protein said it and Millipore intend to establish an "interactive relationship."
 No other details were disclosed about the amount of Millipore's investment in Protein.  Reuter &#3;
