MINING TECHNOLOGY UNIT ENTERS SALES AGREEMENT

<Mining Technology and Resources Inc> said its wholly-owned subsidiary, Mine Support Systems Inc, entered into a sales agreement with <R.M. Wilson Co Inc> of Wheeling, W. Va.
 R.M. Wilson will serve as Mine Support's exclusive sales agent for the company's Mine Roof Support System in the U.S., the company said.  Reuter &#3;
