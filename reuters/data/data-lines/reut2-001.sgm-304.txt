PHONE-A-GRAM <PHOG> NAMES NEW CHAIRMAN

Phone-A-Gram System Inc said it named Eugene Mora its Chairman and chief executive officer.
 Mora succeed Fred Peterson, who resigned.
 Mora had been president of Victor Temporary Services. He has been a member of Phone-A-Gram's board since 1986.  Reuter &#3;
