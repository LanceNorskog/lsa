LORIMAR <LT> GETS OUTBOARD MARINE <OMC> PACT

Lorimar/Telepictures Corp said its Bozell, Jacobs, Kenyon and Eckhardt unit has been named the advertising agency for the Lawn Boy unit of Outboard Marine Corp <OMC>, replacing Walker and Associates.
 Annual billings are estimated at four mln dlrs, it said.  Reuter &#3;
