TRC COS <TRCC> UNIT GETS EPA CONTRACT

TRC Cos Inc said its wholly owned unit, Alliance Technologies Corp, received a 4.2 mln dlr contract from the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency to develop an inventory of pollutants contributing to acid deposition nationwide.
 TRC said the program would be the basis for evaluating the relationship between emission sources and their effects on the environment.  Reuter &#3;
