MCDONNELL DOUGLAS <MD> AWARDS CONTRACTS

McDonnell Douglas Corp said it awarded two contract totaling 284 mln dlrs for landing gear for its new MD-11 jetliner.
 The company said IC Industries Inc's <ICX> Pneumo Abex Corp unit was given a 214 mln dlr contract to make main landing gear, while <AP Precision Hydraulics> was awarded a 70 mln dlr contract to build centerline and nose gear.
 The company said Pneumo's Cleveland Pneumatic unit will build 300 shipsets.
 AP Hydraulics' contract calls for 200 shipsets and options for an additional 100, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
