NABISCO BRANDS LTD <NAB.TO> NAMES NEW CEO

Nabisco Brands Ltd, 80 pct-owned by RJR Nabisco Inc <RJR>, said it named president R. Edward Glover as chief executive, replacing J.R. MacDonald, who remains as vice-chairman and chairman of the executive committee, effective immediately.
 Glover has been president and chief operating officer since April, 1986, Nabisco said.  Reuter &#3;
