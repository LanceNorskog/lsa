AGS COMPUTER <AGS> UNIT GETS IBM LICENSE

Systems Strategies Inc, a unit of AGS Computers Inc, said International Business Machines Corp <IBM> has licensed five of its Systems Network Architecture and Binary Synchronous software packages.
 Systems said it modified the software to run on the IBM 4361 under the IX/370 operating system.  Reuter &#3;
