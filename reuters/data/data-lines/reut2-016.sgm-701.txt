DUPONT'S <DD> CONOCO SAYS NO INTEREST IN DOME

Conoco, a unit of DuPont Co, said it is not interested in buying Dome Petroleum Ltd <DMP>, the Canadian oil company which has said it is in discussions with two unidentified companies.
 "To my knowledge, there is no interest on our part," Conoco spokesman Sondra Fowler said.
 Houston-based Conoco has been mentioned in market speculation on the identity of the two companies. Yesterday, Dome also received a 4.3 billion Canadian dlr offer for all its assets from TransCanada PipeLines Ltd <TMP>.  Reuter &#3;
