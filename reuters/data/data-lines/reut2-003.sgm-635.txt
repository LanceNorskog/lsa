ALLIANT AND MACNEAL-SCHWENDLER SIGN PACT

Alliant Computer Systems Corp <ALNT> said that it has signed an agreement with MacNeal-Schwendler Corp <MNS> where MacNeal will develop a new version of its MSC/NASTRAN software for Alliant's FX/Series Computers.
 Alliant said MSC/NASTRAN is used for finite element analysis, particularly in the automotive and aerospace industries.  Reuter &#3;
