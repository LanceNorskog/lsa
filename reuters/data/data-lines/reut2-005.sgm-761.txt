AERO SERVICES <AEROE> IN PACT FOR NOMINATIONS

Aero Services International Inc said it signed an agreement with Dibo Attar, who controls about 39 pct of its common stock, under which three nominees to Aero's board have been selected by Attar.
 In addition to Attar, the nominees are Stephen L. Peistner, chairman and chief executive officer of <McCrory Corp> and James N.C. Moffat III, vice president and secretary of <Eastover Corp>.  Reuter &#3;
