ALCATEL CIT AND CAP GEMINI WIN SWEDISH CONTRACTS

Alcatel CIT, the French subsidiary of <Alcatel NV> which groups the worldwide telecommunications business of ITT Corp <ITT.N> and France's state-owned Cie Generale d'Electricite <CGE>, has won a 15 mln French franc contract to supply transmission equipment to the Swedish Armed Forces, the company said in a communique here.
 The order is for digital multiplexers.
 At the same time French computer services group <Cap Gemini Sogeti> said it has won a five mln franc contract to develop a data system for the Swedish aeronautical research institute.  REUTER &#3;
