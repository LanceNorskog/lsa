SAFEGUARD SCIENTIFIC <SFE> UNIT BUYS SUBSIDIARY

Safeguard Scientific Inc said its subsidiary, Coherent Communications Systems Corp, purchased a telecommunications equipment business for an undisclosed amount of cash, notes and Coherent common stock.
 Safeguard said it bought the business unit from Comsat TeleSystems Inc, a subsidiary of Communications Satellite Corp <CQ>.  Reuter &#3;
