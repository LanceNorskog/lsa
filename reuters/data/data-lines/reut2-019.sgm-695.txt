ASEA GROUP AWARDED 110 MLN DLR POWER CONTRACT

Asea Group AB <ASEAY> said it has been awarded a 110 mln dlr contract from the Swedish State Power Board and the Finnish utility <Imitran Oma Oy>.
 The company said the contract is for a 500 megawatt 400 kv fenno skan high voltage DC transmission to be built between Sweden and Finland.
 The company also said three other Asea companies; Asea Transmission, Ludvika Sweden and Strondberg Vaasa in Finland, received a related order for converter equipment.  Reuter &#3;
