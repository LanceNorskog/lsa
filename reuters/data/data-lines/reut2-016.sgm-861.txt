SOUTHWEST BANCORP <SWB> HAS HALF OF PREFERRED

Southwest Bancorp said through yesterday, it had receioved 53,571 shares of its 2.50 dlr cumulative convertible preferred stock in response to its offer to exchange 11 common shares for each of the 108,710 shares of the preferred.
 The company said the offer, which was to have expired today, has been extended until May One.  Reuter &#3;
