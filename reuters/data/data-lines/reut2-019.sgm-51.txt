UBS TO SELL 25,000 SANDOZ SHARES

Union Bank of Switzerland (Securities) Ltd said it is lead managing the sale of 25,000 new bearer shares of Sandoz Ltd.
 The shares closed in Zurich yesterday at 12,100 Swiss francs each. Final terms will be set on, or before, June 25.
 The selling concession is two pct while management and underwriting each pays 3/4 pct.  REUTER &#3;
