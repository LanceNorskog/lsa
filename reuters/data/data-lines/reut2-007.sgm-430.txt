GREYHOUND LINES SLASHES FARES

Greyhound Lines said, on its first day under new management, it slashed fares on 166 of its shorter routes, many by 50 pct or more.
 On Wednesday, Greyhound Corp <G> completed the sale of its Greyhound Lines subsidiary to GLI Holdings Inc for 350 mln dlrs in cash, securities, royalties and other considerations.
 Most of the routes affected are 100 miles or less and connect cities and towns throughout the country and include routes serving suburban terminals, colleges, military bases and resort locations.  Reuter &#3;
