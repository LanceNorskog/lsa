C.J. LAWRENCE HAS BUY ON BETHLEHEM STEEL <BS>

Aldo Mazzaferro, analyst at C.J. Lawrence Inc, issued an agressive buy opinion on Bethlehem Steel Corp, traders said.
 Bethlehem rose 7/8 to 10-3/4, the first time in several months the stock has been over 10 dlrs.
 Mazzaferro was not immediately available for comment.  Reuter &#3;
