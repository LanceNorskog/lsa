IVORIAN CHOSEN COCOA COUNCIL EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Edouard Kouame, Ivorian delegate to the International Cocoa Organization (ICCO), was chosen Executive Director of the ICCO, effective October 1, ICCO officials said.
 Kouame will succeed Dr. Kobena Erbynn of Ghana in the post.  REUTER &#3;
