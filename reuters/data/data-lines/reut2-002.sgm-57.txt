<HARD ROCK CAFE PLC> SETS INITIAL U.S. OFFERING

Hard Rock Cafe PLC said it has filed for an initial U.S. offering of 2,240,000 American Depositary Shares representing 11.2 mln Class A ordinary shares.
 It said 240,000 of the ADS's will be sold by a current shareholder.
 Lead underwriter is <Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc>.
 The company said proceeds will be used for the financing of additional restaurants, the expansion of existing restaurants and retail operations and the repayment of debt.  Reuter &#3;
