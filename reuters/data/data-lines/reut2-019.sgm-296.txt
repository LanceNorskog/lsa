METRO AIRLINES <MAIR.O> TO MAKE ACQUISITION

Metro Airlines Inc said it has agreed in principle to acquire privately held Chaparral Airlines inc for 5,700,000 dlrs in cash.
 Chaparral provides regularly scheduled service out of Dallas/Fort Worth Regional Airport as an affiliated carrier of AMR Corp <AMR> and had revenues in 1986 of 14.1 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
