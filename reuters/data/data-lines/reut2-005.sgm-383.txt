CORKEN INTERNATIONAL <CORK> COMPLETES OFFERING

Corken International Corp said it has completed an initial public offering of 500,000 common shares at five dlrs each through underwriters led by Lowell H. Listrom and Co Inc.
 The company said proceeds were used to pay a dividend to Hinderliter Industries Inc <HND>, which had been its sole shareholder, and for working capital.  Reuter &#3;
