FORD <F> SELECTS FIRM TO AUTOMATE PLANT

Ford Motor Co selected <Scicon Corp's> Systems Control unit to install a manufacturing automation system at Ford's Hermosillo, Mexico, plant, Scicon said.
 The system, the value of which was not disclosed, will provide Ford with automated vehicle tracking capabilities, Scicon said.  Reuter &#3;
