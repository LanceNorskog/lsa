BAHRAIN PASSES DECREE TO SET UP STOCK EXCHANGE

Bahrain has passed a decree approving the establishment of the country's first stock exchange, the Gulf News Agency reported.
 The decree placed responsibilty for the stock exchange with the Ministry of Commerce and Agriculture.
 There was no indication when the exchange will open.
 Banking sources said government officials are finalising details of a building to house the exchange.
 Stock trading in Bahrain is currently conducted via a telephone market.  Reuter &#3;
