JEFFERIES MAKES MARKET IN CYCLOPS <CYL>

Jefferies and Co said it is making a market in the stock of Cyclops Corp at 92-1/2 to 95.
 Cyclops received a sweetened offer of 92.50 dlrs per share from Cyacq Acquisition Corp, led by Audio/Video Affiliates Inc <AVA>.
