ATLANTIC RESEARCH <ATRC> WINS MOTOR CONTRACT

Atlantic Research Corp said its propulsion division won a contract valued at 54 mln dlrs from the missiles unit of LTV Corp's <QLTV> LTV Missiles and Electronics Group.
 The contract authorizes the fifth year increment of a multiyear contract to make solid-propellant rocket motors for the U.S. Army's Multiple Launch Rocket System, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
