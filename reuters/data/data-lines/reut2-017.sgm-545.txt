HOUSE OF FABRICS PROJECTS GAINS IN FISCAL 1988

House of Fabrics Inc <HF> said it expects further modest gains in revenues for fiscal year ending January 31, 1988.
 In fiscal 1987, the company's revenues were 316.4 mln dlrs compared to 286.7 mln dlrs the previous fiscal year ending January 31.
 House of Fabrics President and Chief executive, Gary Larkins, said the company will be aggressively promoting sales while applying tighter inventory controls.  Reuter &#3;
