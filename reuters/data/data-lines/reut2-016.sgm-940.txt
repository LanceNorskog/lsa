NORTHERN FOODS TO SELL U.S. UNIT FOR 24 MLN DLRS

Northern Foods Plc <NFDS.L> said its <Northserv Inc> unit had agreed to sell <Flagship Cleaning Services Inc> to <Best Co Inc> of Nevada for 24.6 mln dlrs cash.
 Completion is due on April 30. Flagship is based in Philadelphia and holds the Sears, Roebuck and Co <S> franchise for domestic carpet and upholstery cleaning throughout the U.S.
 In the year to end March, 1986, Flagship - then known as KeyServ - reported pre-tax profits of 2.0 mln dlrs with end-year assets of 8.4 mln, giving a book profit on disposal of 16.2 mln.
 Northern Foods shares were unchanged at 297p.  REUTER &#3;
