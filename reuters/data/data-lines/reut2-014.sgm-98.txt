TELE-COMMUNICATIONS <TCOMA> SELLS CABLE SYSTEMS

Knight-Ridder Inc <KRI> said its TKR Cable Co joint venture with Tele-communications Inc has signed a letter of intent to acquire cable television systems with 55,000 basic subscribers in Wildwood, N.J., and Ramapo and Warwick, N.Y., from Tele-Communications for undisclosed terms.  Reuter &#3;
