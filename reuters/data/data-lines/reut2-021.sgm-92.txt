CCR VIDEO <CCCR.O> IN TALKS ON BEING ACQUIRED

CCR Video Corp said it has received an offer to enter into negotiations for <Intercep Investment Corp> to acquire a controlling interest through a tender offer.
 The company said "The negotiations would determine the terms under which the CCR board of directors could support the INTERCEP tender offer."
 It gave no further details.  Reuter &#3;
