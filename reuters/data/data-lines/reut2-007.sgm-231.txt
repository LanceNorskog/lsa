MCI <MCI> FILES CREDIT CARD FRAUD SUIT

MCI Telecommunications Corp said it filed suit against 27 Johnson C. Smith University students in Mecklenburg County General Court to recover damages from credit card fraud in excess of 600,000 dlrs.
 The suit is based on the use of two credit card codes for long distance services, it said.  Reuter &#3;
