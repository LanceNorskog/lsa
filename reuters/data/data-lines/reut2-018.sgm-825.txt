HYDRO OPTICS <HOPC.O> CHAIRMAN RESIGNS

Hydro Optics Inc said Kenneth S. Roth has resigned as president and chairman. No reason was given.
 It said director Melvin L. Gold has been named chairman and chief executive officer and vice president-sales Barry Kay has been named president and chief operating officer.  Reuter &#3;
