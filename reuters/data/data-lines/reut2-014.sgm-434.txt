JAMESWAY <JMY> SEES YEAR SALES AT 700 MLN DLRS

Jamesway Corp president Arlie Lazarus, said the sales for the fiscal year ending January 30, 1988, would reach approximately 700 mln dlrs.
 Jamesway recorded sales for the fiscal year ending January 31, 1987, of 612.4 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
