POLICY MANAGEMENT <PMSC> TO DEVELOP NEW SYSTEMS

Policy Management Systems Corp said it has begun development of a new generation of systems that will be introduced in early 1988.
 The provider of software to the insurance industry said the Series III systems will offer artificial intelligence and expert systems technology, intelligent workstations, image processing and other capabilities.  Reuter &#3;
