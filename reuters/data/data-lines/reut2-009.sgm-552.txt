BARRIS <BRRS> TO BUY BACK STOCK

Barris Industries Inc said it agreed to buy back 763,546 shares, or 8.6 pct of its own common stock from company founder Charles Barris for 12.50 dlrs per share.
 Barris and the company have also terminated an agreement which granted Barris Industries the right of first refusal for a five year period on any project initiated by Barris, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
