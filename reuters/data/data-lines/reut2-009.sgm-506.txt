CYANOTECH <CYAN> SETS WAY TO EXTRACT CAROTENE

Cyanotech Corp said it has developed a new way to extract beta carotene from algae.
 The company said the method reduces energy requirements by 70 pct and produces 90 pct recovery.
 The company has been a commercial producer of beta carotene since May, 1986.  Reuter &#3;
