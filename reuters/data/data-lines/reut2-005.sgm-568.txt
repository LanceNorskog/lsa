INTERNATIONAL KING'S <IKNG> NAMES PRESIDENT

International King's Table Inc said it named Everett F. Jefferson president and chief operating officer.
 The company said Jefferson was previously president and chief operating officer of Marriott Corp's <MHS> Straw Hat Pizza unit.
 It said he assumes the positions from Roger W. Houmes, who will continue as chairman and chief executive officer of the company, which operates a chain of 82 restaurants in the West.  Reuter &#3;
