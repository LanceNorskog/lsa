FISHER FOODS <FHR> SAYS STAKE IN FIRM SOLD

Fisher Foods Inc said <American Financial Corp> has sold its 1,500,000 Fishers shares, a 44 pct interest, to a group consisting of <American Seaway Foods Inc>, <Rini Supermarkets Inc> and <Rego Supermarkets Inc>.
 The company said in connection with the transaction, all five American Financial representatives have resigned from its board.  Reuter &#3;
