DOW CHEMICAL CO <DOW> UNIT INCREASES PRICES

The Dow Chemical Co said its Engineering Thermoplastics Department will increase the selling prices of standard grades of MAGNUM ABS resins by three cts per pound.
 It also announced an increase of five cts per pound for performance grades of the resins.
 Both increases are effective April 1, 1987.   Reuter &#3;
