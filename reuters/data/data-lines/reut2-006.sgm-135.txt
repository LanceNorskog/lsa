ENDATA <DATA> TERMINATES WORK ON CONTRACT

Endata Inc said it terminated development work on a previously announced contract with <Educational Testing Services>.
 Endata said that under terms of the contract Educational Testing asked it to terminate development of an advanced image processing system integration project and to return all projected-related equipment. Endata said termination of the project will not materially affect its operating results.
 Separately, Endata said it bought the assets and business of three service centers from <Midcom Inc> for an undisclosed amount.  Reuter &#3;
