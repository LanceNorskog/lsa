JACOBS REPLACED AS MINSTAR <MNST> PRESIDENT

Minstar Inc said Kenneth J. Severinson was named president and chief operating officer to succeed Irwin L. Jacobs.
 Jacobs remains chairman and chief executive officer of the company.
 Severinson has most recently worked as president of Minstar's energy services group.  Reuter &#3;
