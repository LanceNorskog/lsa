<ACCUGRAPH CORP> FILES TO REGISTER SHARES

Accugraph Corp said it has filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission relating to 659,800 Class A shares deliverable on the exercise of outstanding warrants.
 It said following the registration, Accugraph will file periodic reports with the SEC required to be filed by companies traded in U.S. markets.  Reuter &#3;
