SAFETY-KLEEN <SK> COMPLETES ACQUISITION

Safety-Kleen Corp said it has completed the acquisition of McKesson Envirosystems Co, a unit of McKesson Corp <MCK> for an undisclosed amount.
 Safety-Kleen, an industrial, automotive parts cleaning service, said McKesson Envirosystems has solvent recycling plants in Illinois, Kentucky and Puerto Rico.  Reuter &#3;
