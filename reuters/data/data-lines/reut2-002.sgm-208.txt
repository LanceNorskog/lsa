TELEMATICS IN INITIAL PUBLIC OFFERING

<Telematics International Inc> said 2,550,000 shares of common at 12 dlrs per share are being offered in an initial public offering.
 It said it is offering 2,050,000 and certain shareholders are offering 500,000 shares.
 <Alex. Brown and Sons Inc> and Robertson, Colman and Stephens are the managers of the syndicate offering the larger number of shares, and Alex. Brown, Colman and Hambros Bank Limited are the managers of the international offering of 500,000 shares.  Reuter &#3;
