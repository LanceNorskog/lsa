ARMSTRONG WORLD <ACK> UNIT BUYS PLANT

Armstrong World Industries Inc said its Gilliam Furniture affiliate has purchased a 130,000 square foot building in Troutman, N.C., for undisclosed terms.
 It said the new plant is expected to be in operation before year-end and and will more than double Gilliam's production capacity for upholstered furniture.  Reuter &#3;
