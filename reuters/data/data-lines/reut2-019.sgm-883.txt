TEXAS INSTRUMENTS GETS 556.1 MLN DLR CONTRACT

Texas Instruments Inc (TXN) has been awarded a 556.1 mln dlr contract to build 2,575 high-speed anti-radiation missiles (HARM), the Navy said.
 The company also has won a 5.9 mln dlr contract to upgrade M-48 tanks for Turkey, the Army said.  REUTER &#3;
