LSB INDUSTRIES <LSB> AGREES TO ACQUIRE BANK

LSB Industries Inc said it agreed to acquire Northwest Federal Savings and Loan Association for 1,500,000 dlrs.
 As part of the agreement, LSB said it also would transfer assets valued of not less than 30 mln dlrs to Northwest Federal, which is located in Woodward, Okla.  Reuter &#3;
