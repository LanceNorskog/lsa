LOS ANGELES SECURITIES <LSGA> TO STAY ON NASDAQ

Los Angeles Securities Group said the <National Association of Securities Dealers> has decided not to delist its securities from the NASDAQ system for now, as it had threatened.
 The company said the NASD has granted it an undetermined period of time to resolve issues raised by the NASD on market-making in its own common stock.  Reuter &#3;
