QUOTRON IN PACT WITH MERRILL LYNCH <MER>

<Quotron Systems Inc> said it signed a contract to deliver financial information services to more than 500 Merrill Lynch Pierce Fenner and Smith offices.
 The agreement provides all the offices served by Quotron be extended through November 1989, and gives Merrill Lynch the option to extend the contract through February 1993, the company said.
 The cost of the contract was not disclosed.
 Quotron is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Citicorp <CCI>, and is not traded publicly.  Reuter &#3;
