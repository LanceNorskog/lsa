NOVAR ELECTRONICS <NOVR> SEES RESULTS IMPROVING

Novar Electronics corp said it expects improved earnings this year due to a rapid expansion of its Logic One computerized buolding management system customer base and expectations of good crime deterrent business.
 The company today reported earnings for the year ended January Three of 207,514 dlrs, up from 98,050 dlrs a year before.  Reuter &#3;
