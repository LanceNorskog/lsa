ROBERT BRUCE <BRUC> DOWNGRADED BY S/P

Standard and Poor's said it lowered Robert Bruce Industries Inc's 15 mln dlrs of subordinated debentures to CCC from B-minus because of a deterioration in earnings and cash flow.
 Debt leverage has risen to 87 pct and the company has suspended dividend payments to conserve cash, S and P noted.  Reuter &#3;
