MICOM SYSTEMS <MICS> ENHANCES MULTIPLEXOR

Micom Systems Inc said it has doubled the data transmission range and speed of its Instalink460 voice/data multiplexor.
 The company said the unit now transmits data up to 23,000 feet at up to 19,200 bits per second and provides synchronous or asynchronous transmission in a single unit.
 Prices for terminal units begin at 195 dlrs and central units at 750 dlrs, it added.  Reuter &#3;
