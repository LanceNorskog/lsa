I.R.E. <IF> FILES SUIT OVER SHARES

I.R.E. Financial Corp said it has filed suit against CenTrust Savings Bank <CTSTP> seeking to compel CenTrust to sell I.R.E. the 289,700 shares of Atlantic Federal Savings and Loan Association <ASAL> that are the subject of an option agreement between the parties.
 The company alleged CenTrust has refused to deliver the shares, claiming that the option had expired.  Reuter &#3;
