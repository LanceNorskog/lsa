EL POLLO ASADO <EPAI.O> NAMES PRESIDENT

El Pollo Asado Inc said executive vice president and chief operating officer Richard J. Peterson has been named president, succeeding Richard B. Lipson, who remains chairman and chief executive.
 The company also said chief financial officer Ronald A. Paradis has been named to the added posts of executive vice president and director.  On the board he replaces Maria A. Lipson.  Reuter &#3;
