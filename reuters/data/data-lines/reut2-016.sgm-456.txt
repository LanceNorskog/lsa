ELECTROHOME <EL.X.TO> TO SELL UNIT

Electrohome Ltd said it agreed to sell certain assets of the computer service sector of its AABEX service division to Canadian General Electric Co Ltd <CGE.TO>.
 Terms were not disclosed. The closing date is expected to be May 1, 1987.  Reuter &#3;
