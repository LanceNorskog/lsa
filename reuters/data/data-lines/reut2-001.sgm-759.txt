HEALTHVEST <HVT> SELLS SHARES

Healthvest, a Maryland real estate investment trust, said it began selling five mln shares of common stock at 21 dlrs a share.
 The company said it is also selling 543,237 shares to Healthcare International Inc <HII>, giving the company a 9.8 pct stake in Healthvest.  Reuter &#3;
