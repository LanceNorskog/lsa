FINANCIAL CORP <FIN> UNIT COMPLETES PURCHASE

Financial Corp of America's American Savings and Loan Association said it completed the previously announced purchase of 16 retail savings branches from Great Western Financial Corp's <GWF> Great Western Savings.
 American Savings said the purchases boost its deposits by about 550 mln dlrs, but do not affect its asset base.  Reuter &#3;
