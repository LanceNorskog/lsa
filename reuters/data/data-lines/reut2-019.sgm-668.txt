PIRELLI GROUP <PIRI.M> UNIT SELLS CABLE

Pirelli group unit Pirelli Cable Corp's communications division said it signed a cable supply contract with NYNEX Corp <NYN> unit, NYNEX Enterprises.
 The company said Pirelli will supply NYNEX with fiber optic cables over the next year. Terms of the contract were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
