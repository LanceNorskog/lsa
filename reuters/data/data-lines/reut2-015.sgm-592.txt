TEXAS AMERICAN <TAE> OMITS PREFERRED PAYOUT

Texas American Energy Corp said its board has decided to again omit the quarterly dividend on its 2.575 dlr cumulative convertible exchangeable preferred stock.
 The dividend would have been payable May One.  The company last paid 64.3 cts a share on the issue in August 1986.  Reuter &#3;
