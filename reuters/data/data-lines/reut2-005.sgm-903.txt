AIRBORNE FREIGHT <ABF> GETS IBM <IBM> CONTRACT

Airborne Freight Corp said it has received a three-year contract to be primary overnight carrier for International Business Machines Corp's domestic air express traffic.
 The company had previously shared IBM domestic traffic with several other carriers.  It said the contract includes overnight and two-day express services for packages 150 pounds and under.  Financial details were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
