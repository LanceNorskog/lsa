BOREALIS IN GOLD EXPLORATION PACT WITH FARAWAY

<Borealis Exploration Ltd> said it entered into an agreement with <Faraway Gold Mines Ltd> of Vancouver, British Columbia, under which Faraway will acquire an interest in Borealis's Whale Cove Gold Property in the Keewatin district of the Northwest Territories.
 Faraway will spend 1.5 mln dlrs on exploration over three years and will hold 50 pct of the property until all expenses are paid out, when the interest will drop to 40 pct.
 Also, Borealis and Faraway will issue treasury shares to each other and Faraway will buy 575,000 Borealis shares from Borealis president Lorne Spence.  Reuter &#3;
