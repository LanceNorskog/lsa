NEW GENERATION PRODUCTS MAKES ACQUISITION

<New Generation Products Inc> said it has acquired a 20 pct ownership in <Personal Protection Technolgies Inc>.
 New Generation said Personal Protection is developing a group of personal care products, which are effective in killing a variety of viruses and bacteria on contact, for U.S. and export markets.  Reuter &#3;
