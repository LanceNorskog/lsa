<ACUSTAR CORP> HAS UNAUTHORIZED ACCOUNT ACTION

Acustar Corp said it has discovered significant unauthorized activity in its corporate accounts but has not yet determined the full extent of the problem.
 It said it has requested that all over-the-counter trading in its stock be halted until it can make a further announcement.  An investigation is underway, it said.  Reuter &#3;
