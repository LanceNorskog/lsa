METROMEDIA BUYS WARRANT FOR SHARES OF ORION

<Metromedia Co> said it purchased from Time Inc's <TL> Home Box Office Inc a warrant to purchase 800,000 shares of common stock of Orion Pictures Corp <OPC>.
 The price for the warrant was 10 mln dlrs, Metromedia said. It added that it now owns 16.4 pct of the outstanding common stock of Orion.  Reuter &#3;
