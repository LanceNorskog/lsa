METROPOLITAN FEDERAL <MFTN> TO MAKE ACQUISITION

Metropolitan Federal Savings and Loan Association said it has signed a letter of intent to acquire American Trust of Hendersonville, Tenn., for an undisclosed amount of cash.
 American Trust had year-end assets of over 40 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
