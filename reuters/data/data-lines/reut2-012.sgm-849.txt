ST GALLEN LAUNCHES 50 TO 60 MLN SWISS FRANC BOND

The Swiss canton of St Gallen is launching a 50 to 60 mln Swiss franc 4-1/4 pct maximum 12-year bond at 100-1/4 pct, lead manager St Gallen Kantonalbank said.
 Subscriptions close April 15 and payment date is April 30.  REUTER &#3;
