UNISYS <UIS> IN MARKETING AGREEMENT WITH VMARK

Unisys Corp said its Value Added Marketing organization signed an agreement with privately held VMark Computer Inc for remarketing of the Unisys 5000 and 7000 series of minicomputers with VMark software.
 Terms were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
