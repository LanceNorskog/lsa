COMPUTER MEMORIES <CMIN.O> TO DELAY MERGER

Computer Memories Inc said its board has decided to take additional time to evaluate the impact of litigation on the proposed acquisition of <Hemdale Film Corp>.
 Computer Memories said it plans to adjourn its annual shareholders meeting, scheduled for October 23, after it is convened. One purpose of the meeting is to consider the transaction, the company explained.
 Computer Memories said Hemdale agrees with this course of action.
