PUNTA GORDA <PGA> CANCELS RIGHTS OFFERING

Punta Gorda Isles Inc said it has cancelled a proposed rights offering to its shareholders due to a previously-announced 7,500,000 dlr private sale of preferred stock to Love Development and Investment Co, which is scheduled to close before the end of the second quarter.
 The company said due to the cancellation, conversion prices for its convertible debentures have returned to their initial levels.  It said it issued no shares or rightsd in connection with the proposed rights offering.  Reuter &#3;
