THAI TIN EXPORTS FALL IN FEBRUARY

Thailand exported 1,120 tonnes of tin metal in February, down from 1,816 tonnes the previous month and 2,140 tonnes a year ago, the Mineral Resources Department said.
 It said major buyers last month were Britain, Japan, the Netherlands, West Germany and the U.S.  REUTER &#3;
