SEOUL THREATENS EMERGENCY MEASURES

The South Korean government will shortly issue a statement warning it will take unspecified emergency measures if widespread protest demonstrations continue, state radio said.
 Yesterday tens of thousands of demonstrators took to the streets of Seoul and other cities, battling riot police and demanding the resignation of President Chun Doo Hwan.  Reuter &#3;
