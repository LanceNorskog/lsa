FAIR LANES <FAIR> SHAREHOLDERS APPROVE MERGER

Fair Lanes Inc said its shareholders approved the previously announced merger with Maricorp Inc, a unit of <Northern Pacific Corp>.
 Under terms of the merger, Fair Lanes said each of its shares of common stock will be converted into the right to receive 1.3043 shares of <BTR Realty Inc>, which is owned by Fair Lines.  Reuter &#3;
