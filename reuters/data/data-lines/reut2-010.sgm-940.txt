<CLAYTON AND DUBILIER INC> SELLS BURPEE

Privately-held Clayton and Dubilier Inc said it has sold W. Atlee Burpee Co to a new company formed by Wicks Capital Corp, Bankers Trust New York Corp <BT> aND Burpee management for undisclosed terms.
 The garden supply company was acquired from ITT Corp <ITT> in December along with O.M. Scott and Sons Co.  Reuter &#3;
