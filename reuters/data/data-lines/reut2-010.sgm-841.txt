NYNEX <NYN> UNIT FILES TO OFFER DEBT SECUTITIES

New York Telephone Co, a unit of NYNEX Corp, said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a shelf registration statement covering up to 500 mln dlrs of debt securities.
 Proceeds will be to refinance outstanding debt or for general corporate purposes, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
