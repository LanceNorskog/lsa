BETA PHASE <BETA.O> LANDS FIRST MAJOR SALE

Beta Phase Inc said it has negotiated the first major sale of its SMArtClamp electronic intravenous infusion device.
 The company said Sharp Memorial Hospital, which participated in clinical tests of the device, has ordered 100 SMArtClamps.  Reuter &#3;
