AETNA LIFE/CASUALTY <AET> FILES TO OFFER NOTES

Aetna Life and Casualty Co said it filed a shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission for the issue of 200 mln dlrs of debt securities.
 Managers of the offering have not been selected. Terms of the securities will be based on market conditions, the company said.
 Proceeds will be added to general funds and used to support Aetna's basic insurance and financial services businesses.  Reuter &#3;
