CBS <CBS> PURCHASES NEW GAME SHOW

<Ralph Andrews Productions> and <CCR  Productions> said their new game show, "Lingo", was purchased for the fall season by CBS Inc for broadcast on its O and O network.
 The show will be hosted by Michael Reagan, son of President Reagan by his marriage to Jane Wyman, the production companies said.  Reuter &#3;
