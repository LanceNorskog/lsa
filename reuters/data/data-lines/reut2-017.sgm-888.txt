FUJITSU GROUP FORECASTS 75 PCT RISE IN NET PROFITS

Fujitsu Ltd <ITSU.T> has forecast that group net profits will rise 75.9 pct in the current year to 38 billion yen and current profits will rise 72.6 pct to 65 billion, the company said in its earnings statement.
 Sales are expected to rise 14 pct to 2,040 billion yen in the same period, it said.  REUTER &#3;
