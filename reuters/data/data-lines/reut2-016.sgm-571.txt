INSITUFORM <IGLSF.O> RAISES WARRANT EXERCISE PRICE

Insituform Group Ltd said the exercise price of its publicly-held warrants will increase 38 cts to 2.76 dlrs per share on April 25 and will increase 38 cts on each succeeding April 25 until their April 25, 1990 expiration.  Reuter &#3;
