FCS <FCSI> RECEIVES MERGER PROPOSAL

FCS Laboratories said its investment banker, Butcher and Singer Inc, received a preliminary merger proposal from another company in the healthcare field.
 FCS said that if various aspects of the proposal are better defined, its board may consider it. FCS said merger negotiations with this other company have been continuing since late August.  Reuter &#3;
