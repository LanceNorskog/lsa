MULTIVEST <MVST> ENDS MERGER TALKS,SETS PURCHASE

Multivest Corp said it has ended talks on <Oryx Capital Corp>'s possible acqusition of Multivest and is starting an offer of 1.51 dlrs a share for all the oustanding shares of <T.B.C. Industries Inc>.
 Multivest said its T.B.C. tender offer is scheduled to expire April 30.  Reuter &#3;
