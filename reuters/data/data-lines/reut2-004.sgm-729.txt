SOCAL EDISON <SCE> UNIT BACK IN SERVICE

Southern California Edison Co said 1.100 megawatt Unit 3 at its San Onofre Nuclear Generating Station is back in service after the completion of a scheduled refueling.
 The unit was taken down January Two.  Other owners include San Diego Gas and Electric Co <SDO> and the cities of Anaheim and Riverside, Calif.  Reuter &#3;
