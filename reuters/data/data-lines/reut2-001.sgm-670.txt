TOYOTA MOTOR U.S.A. FEBRUARY CAR SALES DOWN

Toyota Motor Sales, U.S.A. Inc said its February car sales totaled 36,811, down from 40,012 last February.
 The company said February truck sales totaled 20,437, down from 28,519 a year earlier.
 Toyota said sales of its domestically produced Corolla models totaled 2,098 units during the month.
