KLEER-VU INDUSTRIES TO SELL COSMETICS UNIT

<Kleer-Vu Industries> said the company's board authorized the sale of its Neslemur Co, its cosmetic and toiletries company.
 The company said it is evaluating two separate proposals and will complete the sale within 10 days.  Reuter &#3;
