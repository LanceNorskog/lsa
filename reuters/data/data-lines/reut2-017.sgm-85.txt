CHRYSLER <C> SETS NEW AMC <AMO> TARGET DATE

Chrysler Corp said it and Regie Nationale des Usines Renault <RENA.PA> agreed to set May 5 as the new target date when Chrysler aimed at reaching a definitive agreement for Chrysler for acquire American Motors Corp.
 AMC is 46.1 pct owned by Renault.
 The two companies originally had targeted April 23 as completion date for reaching a definitive agreement.
 Chrysler, which signed a letter of intent on March 9 to acquire AMC, said "considerable progress has been made, but a number of issues remain to be resolved."  Reuter &#3;
