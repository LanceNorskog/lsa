BSD MEDICAL <BSDM> NAMES CHIEF EXECUTIVE

BSD Medical Corp said it named Victor Vaguine as president and chief executive officer.
 He replaces James Skinner, the company said.
 Vaguine was executive vice president of research and engineering at Clini-Therm Corp <CLIN>, the company said.
 In addition, the company said Alan Himber agreed to buy 400,000 dlrs of equity securities by April 30, and agreed to an option to purchase another one mln dlrs by the same date.  Reuter &#3;
