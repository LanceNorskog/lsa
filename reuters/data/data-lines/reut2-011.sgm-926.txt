SHOWBOAT <SBO> GETS APPROVAL FOR LOW-BET DAY

Showboat Inc said the New Jersey Casino control commission has directed its new Altnatic City Showboat Casino, Hotel and Bowling Center to conduct a limited bet day today, in accordance with commission regulations.
 Each new casino must conduct a limited bet day before opening for regular gaming.  Showboat has saiud it expects to open for regular gaming on Friday.  Reuter &#3;
