MERCK <MRK> GETS APPROVAL FOR DOG HEART DRUG

Merck and Co said a drug to prevent heartworm disease in dogs, ivermectin, has been approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration.
 The drug, made by Merck's animal health and agricultural products division, will be sold under the name Heartgard-30 through licensed veterinarians.  Reuter &#3;
