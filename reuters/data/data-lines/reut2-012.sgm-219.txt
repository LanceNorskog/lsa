GRUMMAN <GQ> UNIT WINS MCDONNELL DOUGLAS ORDER

McDonnell Douglas Corp <MD> said it awarded a contract valued at 28 mln dlrs to a unit of Grumman Corp to develop flight control surfaces for the new U.S. Air Force C-17 transport.
 Grumman Aerostructures of Bethpage, N.Y., will design, develop and build the rudders, elevators and other parts for the transports.  Reuter &#3;
