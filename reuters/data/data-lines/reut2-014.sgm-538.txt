PREMDOR PLANS TWO-FOR-ONE STOCK SPLIT

<Premdor Inc> said it planned a two-for-one common stock split, subject to shareholder approval at the May 4 annual meeting.
 It said the split, if approved, would take effect on May 20.
 Premdor manufactures and sells wood doors.  Reuter &#3;
