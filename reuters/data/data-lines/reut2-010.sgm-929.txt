SIERRACIN <SER> PAYS DEBT, GETS NEW LOAN PACT

Sierracin Corp said it completed the repayment of its entire 14 mln dlr bank debt and entered a new, unsecured revolving credit term loan agreement.
 The new arrangement provides for borrowings of up to 10 mln dlrs at the bank's prime rate and replaces a secured credit line which expired in February, Sierracin also said.  Reuter &#3;
