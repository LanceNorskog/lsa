LE PEEP RESTAURANTS SUES OHIO COMPANY

<Le Peep Restaurants Inc> said it is suing an Ohio company, charging it copied its restaurant from its menu to its design.
 The action is being taken against <Shelly's Inc>, operator of three restaurants in Columbus, Ohio, the company said. The suit alleges copyright infringement and unfair competition, it said.
 The suit was filed in U.S. District Court of the Southern District in Ohio, and seeks damages in excess of 2,250,000, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
