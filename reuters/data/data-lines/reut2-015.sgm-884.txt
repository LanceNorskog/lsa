FLUOROCARBON <FCBN> BUYS EATON <ETN> DIVISION

Fluorocarbon Co said it signed a definitive agreement to acquire Eaton Corp's Industrial Polymer division for an undisclosed price.
 The polymer division, based in Aurora, Ohio, had 1986 sales of 86 mln dlrs, Fluorocarbon also said.  Reuter &#3;
