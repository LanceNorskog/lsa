MFS MULTIMARKET <MMT> INITIAL OFFERING UNDERWAY

Lead underwriters Wheat, First Securities Inc and E.F. Hutton Group Inc <EFH> said an initial public offering of 110 mln shares of beneficial interest of MFS Multimarket Income Trust is under way at 10 dlrs per share.
 The trust has granted underwriters an option to buy up to 16.5 mln more shares to cover overallotments.  Reuter &#3;
