WASHINGTON ENERGY <WECO> PLANS PUBLIC OFFERING

Washington Energy Co said it plans to file a registration statement later this month covering a planned public offering of 1.2 mln common shares.
 Proceeds from the offering will be used to reduce short-term bank borrowings incurred to finance capital expenditures, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
