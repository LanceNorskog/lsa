COMPUTER NETWORK TECH <CMNT> SIGNS PACT

Computer Network Technology Corp (CNT) said it signed an equipment maintenance agreement with Storage Technology Corp <QSTK> that would allow Storage to provide field support and maintenance in the U.S. for Computer Network Technology products.
 CNT designs, manufactures and markets products for high speed networking systems.  Reuter &#3;
