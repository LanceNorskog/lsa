MAGMA POWER <MGMA> NAMES NEW CHIEF EXECUTIVE

Magma Power Co said director Arnold L. Johnson has been named president and chief executive officer.
 He succeeds Andrew W. Hoch, who moved to chairman in February following the resignation of B.C. McCabe Sr.
 The company said Johnson has also been named president and chief executive officer of Magma Energy Inc <MAGE>, which Magma Power controls, succeeding Hoch, who becomes Magma Energy chairman as well. McCabe, whom Hoch also succeeds there, has been named chairman emeritus.  Reuter &#3;
