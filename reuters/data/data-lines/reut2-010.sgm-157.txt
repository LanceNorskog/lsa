AMERICANTURE <AAIX> BUYS AMERICAN ADVENTURE

Americanture Inc said it has purchased American Adventure Inc <GOAQC> for cash, the assumption of liabilities and the issuance of American Adventure Inc common and preferred stock to creditors, shareholders and members.
 The acquisition was pursuant of a Chapter 11 reorganization plan of American Adventure.
 The company said the transaction involved assets valued at more than 83 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
