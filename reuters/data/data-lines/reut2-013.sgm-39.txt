TRIBUNE <TRB> FILES 300 MLN DLR SHELF REGISTRATION

Tribune Co said it filed a shelf registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission for 300 mln dlrs in debt securities.
 Underwriters may include Salomon Bros Inc and Merrill Lynch, it said. Proceeds will be for general needs.  Reuter &#3;
