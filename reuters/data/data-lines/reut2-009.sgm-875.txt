VISHAY <VSH> COMPLETES EXCHANGE OFFER

Vishay Intertechnology Inc said it has received a total of 2,362,616 common shares in response to its offer to exchange Class B shares for common shares, and all common shares received were accepted.
 Vishay said about 737,500 shares were tendered by chairman Alfred Slaner and 1,164,607 by president Felix Zandman, with other executives tendering another 223,387 shares.
 Vishay said it now has 4,675,435 common shares outstanding.  Reuter &#3;
