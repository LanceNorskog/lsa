LANDMARK SAVINGS <LSA> TO REPURCHASE SHARES

Landmark Savings Association said its board has authorized the repurchase of up to 100,000 shares, or four pct of its outstanding stock, from time to time over the next 12 months.
 It said purchases are expected to start around May Four.  Reuter &#3;
