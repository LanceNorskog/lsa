MERIDIAN DIAGNOSTICS <KITS> HAS TYPHUS TEST

Meridian Diagnostics Inc said it has developed a rapid diagnostic test for endemic or murine typhus infections caused by Rickettsii typhi and transmitted by flea bites.
 It said the product will be sold to hospitals, physicians, commerical laboratories and veterinarians by <Integrated Diagnostics Inc>.  Reuter &#3;
