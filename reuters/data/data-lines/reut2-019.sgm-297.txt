WEATHERFORD INTERNATIONAL <WII> EXECUTIVE LEAVES

Weatherford International Inc said executive vice president and chief operating officer Kurt K. Bushati will leave September One to accept a senior operating position with the Austrian national oil company.
 The company said president and chief executive officer Eugene L. Butler will assume the added duties of chief operating officer.  Reuter &#3;
