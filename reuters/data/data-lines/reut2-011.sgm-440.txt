HANSON TRUST <HAN> SELLS UNIT

Reichhold Chemicals Inc said it is part of a group that has purchased PCR Inc from Hanson Trust PLC's SCM Corp subsidiary.
 It said other group members include Jacksonville, Fla., businessman Jack C. Demetree and managers of PCR.
 Reichhold said it made a "modest" cash investment.  PCR makes high-performance materials for a variety of industries.  Reuter &#3;
