IRAN'S NON-OIL EXPORTS RISE 35 PCT IN YEAR

Iran's non-oil exports were worth at least 750 mln dlrs in the year ended March 20, a 35 pct increase from the previous year, the national Iranian news agency IRNA reported.
 IRNA, monitored in London, quoted a senior Industry Ministry official as saying non-oil exports included carpets, animal skins, pistachios, fruit and industrial goods.  REUTER &#3;
