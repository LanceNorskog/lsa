PORSCHE CARS NORTH AMERICA REPORTS LOWER SALES

Porsche Cars North America said it sold 1,815 cars during February, down from 2,129 cars during February of 1986.
 The company said sales since the beginning of the year totaled 3,469, down from 3,862 a year earlier.  Reuter &#3;
