CAREPLUS <CPLS> SEEKS APPROVAL IN ACQUISITION

CarePlus Inc said it will seek shareholder approval of its proposed acquisition of certain assets and assumption of certain liabilities of Professional Care Inc <PCI>.
 In connection with the acquisition, shareholders will be requested to approval the merger of CarePlus into its newly formed Delaware subsidiary, CarePlus said.
 CarePlus said its board has tentatively determined that its stock would be exchanged for common stock of the new corporation at the rate of seven for one.  Reuter &#3;
