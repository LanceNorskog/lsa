US WEST <USW> UNIT WINS POINT-OF-SALE CONTRACT

US West Information Systems said it completed an agreement with the Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Ltd <ANZA.S> to provide a turnkey installation of hardware, software and support services for a nationwide point-of-sale network in Australia.
 Financial terms of the five-year agreement were not disclosed. US West Information Systems is a unit of US West Inc.  Reuter &#3;
