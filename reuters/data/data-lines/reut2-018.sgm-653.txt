LOMAS AND NETTLETON <LNF> TO MAKE ACQUISITION

Lomas and Nettleton Financial Corp said it has agreed in principle to acquire Houston discount brokerage firm Texas First Brokerage Services Inc for undisclosed terms, subject to regulatory approvals.
 It said completion is expected by July 31.  Reuter &#3;
