RETAIL CHAIN TO SELL ROCKY MOUNTAIN <RMED> ITEM

Rocky Mountain Medical Corp said the Baby News Store chain of children's stores will carry its tenderCare line of non-chemical disposable diapers.
 Rocky claimed its patented dryness technology provides a diaper that is demonstrably drier than conventional disposable diapers and cloth diapers.
 Baby News' 60 stores are located in the western U.S.  Reuter &#3;
