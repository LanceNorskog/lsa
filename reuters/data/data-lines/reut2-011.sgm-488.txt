CARTER-DAY IN SETTLEMENT WITH POWER COMPANIES

Carter-Day INdustries Inc said it reached an eight mln dlrs settlement in a suit filed in the Bankruptcy Court for the Southern District of N.Y. against five power companies.
 The suit was filed against Montana Power Co <MTP>, Puget Sound Power and Light Co <PSD>, Portland General Electric Co <PGN>, Washington Water Power Co <WWP> and PacifiCorp's <PPW> Pacific Power and Light Co.
 Carter-Day said proceeds from the settlement will be used to restore the company's liquidity, working capital and net worth.  Reuter &#3;
