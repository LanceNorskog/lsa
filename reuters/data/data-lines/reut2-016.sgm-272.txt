TEXACO CANADA <TXC> UNAFFECTED BY LEGAL MOVE

Texaco Canada Inc said its business operations, its plans and financial integrity are unaffected by yesterday's move by 78 pct-owner Texaco Inc <TX> to file for protection under U.S. bankruptcy law.
 Texaco Canada chief executive Peter Bijur said in a statement that "it is business as usual for us on Monday morning."
 Texaco Inc added in a statement that it "has never operated outside the U.S., and relations with all foreign governments and activities of interest to them and their citizens are not affected" by its legal dispute with Pennzoil Co <PZL>.  Reuter &#3;
