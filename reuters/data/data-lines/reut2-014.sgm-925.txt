ITALY'S LA FONDIARIA TO REPORT HIGHER 1986 PROFITS

Italian insurer La Fondiaria Spa <LFDI.MI> said it expects to report consolidated group profit in 1986 significantly higher than the 60 billion lire reported in 1985.
 The company said in a statement that parent company net profit last year will rise from the 72 billion lire reported in 1985. Consolidated group premiums totaled 1,700 billion lire in 1986 compared with 1,490 billion the previous year.
 Iniziativa Meta <INZI.MI>, the financial services unit of Montedison Spa <MONI.MI>, controls the largest single stake in Florence-based Fondiaria with 49.9 pct.  REUTER &#3;
