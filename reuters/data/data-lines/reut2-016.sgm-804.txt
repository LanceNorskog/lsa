CHEMLAWN <CHEM> POSTPONES ANNUAL MEETING

ChemLAwn Corp said due to the tender offer by Ecolab Inc <ECL> for ChemLawn shares at 36.50 dlrs each, it has postponed its annual meeting, which had scheduled for April 23.
 No new date was set.  The tender offer is scheduled to expire April 20 unless extended.  Reuter &#3;
