FORTUNE SAVINGS TO BUY FINANCIAL <FSSL> BRANCH

<Fortune Savings Bank> said it agreed to buy the deposits and assume the leasehold of a Financial Security Savings and Loan Association <FSSL> branch here.
 Terms of the agreement were not disclosed.
 Fortune said the agreement is subject to regulatory approval. It also said that if the transaction is approved, Financial would generate a profit on the sale.  Reuter &#3;
