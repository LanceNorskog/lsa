NEXXUS <NEXX> SIGNS CONTRACTS WORTH 10 MLN DLRS

Nexxus Technologies Inc said it has signed production contracts totaling about 10 mln dlrs with three major U.S. gem wholesalers.
 The company said it expects to start operations on these contracts in May, adding the work will continue through late 1989. Additional contracts with international gem wholesalers are expected in the near future, it added without providing details.  Reuter &#3;
