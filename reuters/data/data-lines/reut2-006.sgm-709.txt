CROSS AND TRECKER <CTCO> TO SELL UNIT

Cross and Trecker Corp said its Warner and Swasey subsidiary will seek to sell its Grinding Division to focus on other areas of its business.
 The company said the Grinding Division had sales last year of about 18 mln dlrs. It makes grinding machines.  Reuter &#3;
