HOLLINGER <HLG.TO> BUYS CANADIAN MAGAZINE

Hollinger Inc said it purchased Saturday Night, a Canadian general-interest magazine.
 Financial terms were not disclosed, but Hollinger said it has no immediate plans for changes in senior editorial personnel.
 Saturday Night, which publishes features and analyses of current events, is celebrating its 100th anniversary and has a circulation of about 120,000, according to a magazine spokesman.
 Hollinger, which owns 58 pct of <Daily Telegraph PLC>, also today completed the acquisition of Unimedia Inc, a Quebec-based publisher.  Reuter &#3;
