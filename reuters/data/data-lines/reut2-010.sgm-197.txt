ACME-CLEVELAND <AMT> CEO TO STEP DOWN

B. Charles Ames, chairman of Acme-Cleveland Corp, said he will step down as chief executive officer of the company.
 He will be replaced by David Swift, who was elected to serve as president and chief executive officer, effective April 1, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
