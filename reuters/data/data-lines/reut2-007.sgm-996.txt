JOHN HANCOCK UNITS DUMP STAKE IN PRICE <PR>

Subsidiaries of John Hancock Mutual Life Insurance Co told the Securities and Exchange Commission they sold their entire 13.1 pct stake in Price Communications Corp back to the company.
 The Hancock subsidiaries said they sold their entire 1,627,603-share stake in Price Communications to the company for 11 dlrs a share on Feb 23.  Reuter &#3;
