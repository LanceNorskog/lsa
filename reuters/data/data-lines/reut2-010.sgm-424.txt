IC INDUSTRIES<ICX> UP AFTER BEAR STEARNS REPORT

IC Industries Inc rose 2-3/4 to 33-1/4 following a favorable comment by analyst Gary Schneider of Bear Stearns and Co, traders said.
 Schneider was not immediately available for comment.
 IC Industries also was the subject of a favorable report earlier in the week from First Boston Corp, traders said.
 Sources said Schneider's report concluded IC Industries shares are undervalued and will eventually trade in the mid-40's.  Reuter &#3;
