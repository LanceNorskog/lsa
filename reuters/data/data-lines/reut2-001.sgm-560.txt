OUTBOARD MARINE <OM> KNOWS NO REASON STOCK IS UP

Outboard Marine Corp, whose stock is trading higher, said there were no corporate developments to account for the rise.
 Outboard Marine was up 2-1/2 to 34 with 48,000 shares traded.
 A spokesman said the company wasn't planning any announcements. Several analysts who follow the company also did not know why Outboard was trading higher.  Reuter &#3;
