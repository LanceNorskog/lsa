U.S. TO SELL 13.2 BILLION DLRS IN BILLS

The U.S. Treasury said it will sell 13.2 billion dlrs of three and six-month bills at its regular auction next week.
 The March 9 sale, to be evenly divided between the three and six month issues, will result in a paydown of 2.45 billion dlrs as maturing bills total 15.65 billion dlrs.
 The bills will be issued March 12.  Reuter &#3;
