Memtek Corp said it has sold 1,500,000 shares privately through underwriter Allen and Co Inc for 1,275,000 dlrs.
 It said proceeds will be used to expand its development and commercialization of bioactive membrane products and waste water treatment systems.  Reuter &#3;
