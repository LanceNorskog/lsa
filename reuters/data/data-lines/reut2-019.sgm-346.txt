MARS GRAPHIC <WMD> SEES HIGHER 2ND QTR SALES

Mars Graphic Services Inc said it expects sales for the second quarter ending August 31 to exceed year ago sales of 3.7 mln dlrs for the same period.
 In the year-ago quarter the company earned 191,000 dlrs or 18 cts a share.  Reuter &#3;
