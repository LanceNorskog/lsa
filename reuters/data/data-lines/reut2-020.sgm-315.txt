WHIRLPOOL <WHR> DISCUSSING JOINT VENTURE

Whirlpool Corp said it is having joint venture discussions with <Vitro S.A.> of Monterrey, Nuevo Leon, Mexico, on the formation of a Mexican venture for the manufacture and sale of major home appliances.
 It said Vitro has consolidated sales projected at 1.0 billion dlrs (U.S.) for 1987.  Reuter &#3;
