CALLON PETROLEUM <CLNP> GETS QUALIFIED AUDIT

Callon Petroleum Co said its auditors will qualify their opinion on 1986 financial statements due to questions about Callon's ability to continue as a going concern.
 The company said negotiations to restructure its debt and allow for continued operations are proceeding.
 Today it reported a loss for 1986 of 4.20 dlrs per share, compared with a year earlier loss of 4.71 dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
