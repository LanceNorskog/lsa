STEEL TECHNOLOGIES <STTX>, MITSUI SET VENTURE

Steel Technologies Inc said it agreed to form a 50-50 joint venture with <Mitsui and Co Ltd> to make steel products.
 The venture, called Mi-Tech Steel Inc, will be set up to serve Japanese and domestic automobile and appliance parts makers in the U.S.
 A plant will be located near Murfreesboro, Tenn., and production is expected to begin in the fall 1987, the company said.   Reuter &#3;
