FRONTIER AIRLINES SEEKS FASTER CLAIM SETTLEMENT

Continental Airlines Inc said Frontier Airlines, which it bought last year, has sought approval from the U.S. Bankruptcy court here to pay up to 1.9 mln dlrs in employee medical, dental and other benefit claims on an expedited basis.
 Continental is itself owned by Texas Air Corp <TEX>.
 Some 1.2 mln dlrs of the claims would be paid directly to former Frontier employees for medical expenses incurred prior to, and immediately after, Fronter filed for bankrupcty and ceased operations in August last year.  REUTER &#3;
