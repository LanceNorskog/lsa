<MONTCLAIR SAVINGS BANK> INITIAL OFFERING STARTS

Lead underwriters PaineWebber Group Inc <PWJ> and Ryan, Beck and Co Inc <RBCO> said an initial public offering of 537,500 shares of Montclair Savings Bank common stock is under way at 15 dlrs per share.
 Underwriters have been granted an option to purchase up to an additional 322,500 shares to cover overallotments.
 The company also sold 1,612,500 shares in a subscription and community offering.  Reuter &#3;
