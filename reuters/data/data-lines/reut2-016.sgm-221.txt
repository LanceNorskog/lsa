JANUARY NEWPAPER ADVERTISING INCOME UP 10.5 PCT

The Newspaper Advertising Bureau said its preliminary estimates show spending for newspaper advertising in January totaled 2.06 billion dlrs, an increase of 10.5 pct from the year earlier month.
 The bureau said retail advertising for the month was up 10.7 pct to 1.02 billion dlrs. National advertising was off 0.7 pct to 264 mln dlrs. Classified increased 14.7 pct to 777 mln dlrs.  Reuter &#3;
