HUMANA <HUM> TO SELL MEDICAL OFFICES

Humana Inc said it has agreed in principle to sell 68 MedFirst primary medical care facilities to <Primedical Corp> for undisclosed terms, with transfers taking place over the next four months.
 It said it retains 37 MedFirst offices, mostly in the Chicago area.
 The transaction is not expected to have any impact on earnings, Humana said.  Reuter &#3;
