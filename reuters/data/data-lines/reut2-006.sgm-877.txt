AMERICAN INTERNATIONAL <AIPN> IN SUIT

American International Petroleum Corp said a law suit alleging breach of contract and misrepresentation has been filed against its Majestic Crude Corp subsidiary by AMONI Ltd.
 The suit seeks 600,000 dlrs in damages.
 American International said it will defend against the charges.  Reuter &#3;
