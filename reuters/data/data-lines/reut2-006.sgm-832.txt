LITTON <LIT> UNIT GETS 164.2 MLN DLR CONTRACT

Litton Industries' Applied Technology Division is being awarded a 164.2 mln dlr Navy contract for aircraft radar warning receivers for the U.S. Navy, Australia and Spain, the Defense Department said.
 It said work on the contract, which combines purchases for the three countries under the Foreign Military Sales program, is expected to be completed in Febraury 1990.  Reuter &#3;
