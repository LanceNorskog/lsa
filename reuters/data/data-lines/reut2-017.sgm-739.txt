NATIONAL HEALTHCARE <NHC> OPENS CENTER

National HealthCorp LP said it has opened a 120-bed nursing care and phsical therapy center in Pensacola, Fla., called Palm Gardens of Pensacola.
 It said it manages the center for owner Florida Convalescent Centers Inc.
