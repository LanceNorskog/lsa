LIBERIAN SHIP GROUNDED IN SUEZ CANAL REFLOATED

A Liberian motor bulk carrier, the 72,203 dw tonnes Nikitas Roussos, which was grounded in the Suez canal yesterday, has been refloated and is now proceeding through the the canal, Lloyds Shipping Intelligence said.  Reuter &#3;
