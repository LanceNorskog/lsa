WESTERN MINING SELLS ACM STAKE

Western Mining Corp Holdings Ltd <WMNG.S> (WMC) said it sold its entire holding of 22.13 mln shares in the gold mining company <Australian Consolidated Minerals Ltd> (ACM).
 WMC gave no details, but stockbrokers said the sale was made at eight dlrs a share on Friday to a number of U.S., European and Australian investors through WMC's usual brokers.
 The company purchased the 19.9 pct parcel in early March from Amax Inc <AMX.N> at 6.32 dlrs a share ahead of a one-for-three bonus issue when Amax sold its entire 47 pct stake in ACM to a number of local companies.  REUTER &#3;
