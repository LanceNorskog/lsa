MAJOR VIDEO <MAJV.O> FILES 1.4 MLN SHR OFFER

Major Video Corp said it filed a registration statement covering a proposed public offering of 1.4 mln shares of common stock to be sold by the company.
 It said Rauscher Pierce Refsnes Inc will be managing underwriter of the offering, expected in July, with proceeds to be used to finance development and possible acquisition of additional Major Video Superstores and for working capital.  Reuter &#3;
