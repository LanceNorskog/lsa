RAPID-AMERICAN COMPLETES K MART <KM> STORE BUY

Privately-held Rapid-American Corp said it has completed the previously-announced acquisition of 66 Kresge and Jupiter stores from K Mart Corp.
 The company said it plans to operate 57 of the stores as McCrory Five and 10 variety stores and close the others by the end of July.  Reuter &#3;
