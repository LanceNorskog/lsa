U.S. BILL SALES SEEN AT 5.74/72, 5.82/81 PCT

The U.S. Treasury's regular weekly bill auction is expected to produce an average yield of about 5.74/72 pct for the three-month maturities and 5.82/81 pct for the six-month, dealers said.
 The current issues were yielding 5.71/70 and 5.76/75 pct respectively in early afternoon trading.  Reuter &#3;
