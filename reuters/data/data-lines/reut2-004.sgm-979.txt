H.K. PORTER <PHK PR> TO REDEEM PREFERRED STOCK

H.K. Porter Inc Co said it will redeem its 5-1/2 pct cumulative sinking fund preference stock.
 H.K. Porter said it will provide more details about the redemption later in the day.
 The company's stock was halted in trading on the NYSE at 97-1/4.  Reuter &#3;
