UNC RESOURCES <UNC> TO EXPAND FACILITY

UNC Inc said it has reached agreement with the U.S. Department of Energy to expand production facilities at the company's Naval Products Division in Uncasville, Conn.
 The company said the 55 mln dlr project will start immediately and is scheduled for completion in 1990.  Reuter &#3;
