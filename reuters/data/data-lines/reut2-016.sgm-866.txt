DIAMOND SHAMROCK <DIA> SETS DATES FOR SPINOFF

Diamond Shamrock Corp said it has set April 30 as the record date for the spinoff of its refining and marketing operations, Diamond R and M Inc, and shares will be mailed around May 10.
 Shareholders will receive one Diamond R and M share for each four Diamond Shamrock shares held.  Reuter &#3;
