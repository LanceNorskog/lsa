<GRAND UNION CO> SEES HIGHER YEAR SPENDING

Grand Union Co, a subsidiary of Generale Occidentale SA of France, said it plans capital spending of 111 mln dlrs this year mostly for new stores and expansions of existing units, up from 76 mln dlrs for the year ended March 28, 1987.  Reuter &#3;
