A.H BELO <BLC> TO REINCORPORATE

A.H. Belo Corp said it will submit to its shareholders at its May six annual meeting a proposal to reincorporate the company in Delaware.
 The company said the reincorporation would enable Belo to take advantage of Delaware's flexible corporate laws.  Reuter &#3;
