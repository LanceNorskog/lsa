UAL INC <UAL>'S UNITED AIRLINES ADDS FLIGHTS

UAL Inc's United Airlines said it is adding flights into Los Angeles International Airport between now and June.
 United said it will be adding flights between Los Angeles and  Phoenix, Ariz., Oakland, Calif., Sacramento, Calif., Chicago and  Salt Lake City.  Reuter &#3;
