PAKISTAN FIRM RAISES THREE BILLION YEN IN JAPAN

The National Development Finance Corp of Pakistan has raised three billion yen through private placement, arranger Daiwa Securities Co Ltd said.
 The seven year "Shibosai" issue in the Tokyo domestic market carries interest at 6.9 pct. No other details were available.  REUTER &#3;
