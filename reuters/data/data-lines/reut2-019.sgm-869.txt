CYCARE SYSTEMS INC <CYCR> BUYS MEDTEC

CyCare Systems Inc said it bought Control Data Corp's <CDA> MedTec unit for an undisclosed sum.
 The company said MedTec's new client base could have a significant impact on long-term revenues, although it expects a minimal positive impact on earnings in the next six months.
 It said the assets purchased include processing agreements, software maintenance contracts and the supporting software.  Reuter &#3;
