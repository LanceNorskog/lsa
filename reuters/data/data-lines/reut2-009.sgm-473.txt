AIM TELEPHONES <AIMT> SELLS DEBT TO SHAREHOLDER

AIM Telephones Inc said it has sold three mln dlrs of nine pct seven-year subordinated notes to principal shareholder <Quince Associates>.
 The company said it has also issued Quince seven-year warrants to buy 500,000 common shares at six dlrs each.  Reuter &#3;
