SKYLARK TO ISSUE 20 BILLION YEN CONVERTIBLE BOND

Skylark Co Ltd <SKLK.T> said it will issue a 20 billion yen nine-year unsecured convertible bond in the domestic capital market through public placement with Nomura Securities Co Ltd as lead manager.
 Coupon and conversion price for par-priced bond maturing June 28, 1996, will be set at its board meeting to be held in mid-June and payment is due on July 1, the company said in a statement. Skylark's share price fell 10 yen to 2,880 on the Tokyo Stock Exchange today.  REUTER &#3;
