AMERICAN EXPRESS <AXP> UNIT IN FUNDING PROGRAM

American Express Co's Shearson Lehman Brothers said it helped establish a national pooled capital program offering low-cost financing to small and medium-sized businesses.
 Shearson said that it and non-profit <American Development Finance Corp> will help organize the Enterprise Capital Fund, which will initially have a 100 mln dlr pool.
 The capital will be used primarily to make loans that finance small business growth and other projects that contribute to job creation and economic develpment, Shearson said.  Reuter &#3;
