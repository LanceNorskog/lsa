KAYDON <KDON.O> NAMES NEW CHAIRMAN

Kaydon Corp said president Richard A. Shantz has been named chairman, succeeding Glenn W. Bailey, who remains on the board.
 The company said Lawrence J. Cawley, who had been president of the Bearing Division, has ben named to succeed Shantz as Kaydon president.  Reuter &#3;
