H.J. HEINZ <HNZ> ELECTS NEW CHAIRMAN

H.J. Heinz Co said its board of directors has elected Anthony O'Reilly chairman of the board, succeeding Henry Heinz II, who died on February 23.
 Heinz said O'Reilly will retain his present titles of president and chief executive officer.  Reuter &#3;
