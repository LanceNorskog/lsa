IRAN ANNOUNCES NEW OFFENSIVE IN GULF WAR

Iran announced it had launched a new offensive against Iraq in the north-west area of the Gulf war front.
 Tehran radio, monitored by the British Broadcasting Corporation, said the attack was launched last night in the Haj Omran area of northern Iraq.
 It had already achieved "considerable victories," the radio said.  REUTER &#3;
