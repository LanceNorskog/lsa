NORTH AMERICAN PHILIPS <NPH> UNIT NAMES EXECUTIVE

North American Philips Corp said Lyman Beggs has been named president of its Consumer Products Divisions.
 The company said Beggs, formerly executive vice president of Tambrands Inc <TMB>, succeeds Richard Kress, who retired.
 The company said approximately 58 pct of the common stock of North American Philips is owned by N.V. Philips of the Netherlands.  Reuter &#3;
