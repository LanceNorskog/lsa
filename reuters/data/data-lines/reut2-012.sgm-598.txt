MORTON THIOKOL <TKI> UNIT TO MARKET RESINS

Morton Thiokol Inc said its Morton Chemical Division entered into an agreement with <Mitsubishi Chemical Industries Ltd>, Japan, to market extrudable adhesive resins based on Mitsubishi's Novatec AP product line technology.
 It said the resins are used in a broad range of applications, including barrier co-extrusion with nylon and Ethylene-Vinylalcohol Copolymer for film, sheet and bottles.  Reuter &#3;
