INTERNATIONAL TECHNOLOGY <ITX> MAKES ACQUISITION

International Technology Corp said it has purchased <Western Emergency Services Inc> in a pooling-of-interests transaction.
 International Technology, a hazardous materials management company, said it purchased Western Emergency, an environmental services firm, to offer a broader range of environmental services to the Gulf Coast area.  Reuter &#3;
