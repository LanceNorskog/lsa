PERINI INVESTMENT <PNV> BUYS FLORIDA PROPERTIES

Perini Investment Properties Inc said it has purchased three industrial/warehouse properties in Boca Raton, Fla., and one in Pompano Beach, Fla., for an undisclosed amount.
 The company said the purchases increase its industrial/warehouse inventory by about 252,000 square feet.  Reuter &#3;
