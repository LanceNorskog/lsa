MADEIRA IN LETTER OF INTENT TO BE ACQUIRED

<Madeira Inc> said it signed a letter of intent to be acquired by Tradevest Inc through a stock-for-stock exchange.
 After completion of the transaction, Tradevest would own 90 pct of the issued outstanding stock of Madeira.  Reuter &#3;
