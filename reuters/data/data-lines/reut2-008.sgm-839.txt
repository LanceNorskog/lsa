OLIVETTI ADDS COMPUTER, SOFTWARE TO PRODUCT LINE

Ing C. Olivetti EC Spa <OLIV.MI> said a new computer and software have been added to its 3B Unix computer line, which is developed by American Telephone and Telegraph Co <A> and distributed by Olivetti outside the U.S.
 Olivetti said in a statement that the new computer is equipped with higher storage capacity and can support up to 64 simultaneous users.
 The new software will improve connections between 3B systems and other manufacturers' systems and simplify display and printing capabilities in various languages, Olivetti said.  Reuter &#3;
