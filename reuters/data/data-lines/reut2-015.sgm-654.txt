HEALTH/REHABILITATION <HRP> SETS FIRST PAYOUT

Health and Rehabilitation Properties Trust said its board declared an initial dividend of 55 cts, including 50 cts for the first quarter just ended and five cts from its initial operating period that began December 23.
 The dividend is payable May 30, record April 20.  Reuter &#3;
