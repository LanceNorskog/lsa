SONEX RESEARCH <SONX> SELLS STOCK PRIVATELY

Sonex Research Inc said it has completed a private sale of common shares and warrants for two mln dlrs.
 It said if all warrants are exercised it will receive another 4,600,000 dlrs.
 Proceeds will be used for working capital, it said.  Reuter &#3;
