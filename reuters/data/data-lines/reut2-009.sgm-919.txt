CONTEL <CTC> TO BUY WALKER COUNTY TELEPHONE

Contel Corp said it has agreed in principle to acquire <Walker County Telephone Co> of LaFayette, Ga., for an undisclosed amount of common stock.
 Walker has 7,600 customers in northeast Georgia.
 The company said the agreement is subject to approval by regulatory agencies, both boards and Walker shareholders.  Reuter &#3;
