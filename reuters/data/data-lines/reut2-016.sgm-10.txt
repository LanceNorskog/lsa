TENNEX INDUSTRIES TO BUILD PLANT IN TENNESSEE

<Tennex Industries Inc>, a Japanese owned supplier of air cleaners for the automotive industry, said it will build a seven mln dlr plant in Munfreesboro, Tenn.
 The company said the 37,500-square-foot plant will initially supply parts to <Nissan Motor Manufacturing Corp USA> in nearby Smyna.
 The company said it hopes to eventually produce parts for other U.S. car makers.  Reuter &#3;
