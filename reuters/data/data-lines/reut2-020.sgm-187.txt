MASCO <MASK.O> TO BUY UP TO 10 MLN SHARES

Masco Industries Inc said its board authorized the repurchase of up to 10 mln of the company's common stock in open market purchases, privately negotiated transactions or otherwise.
 As of June 30, the company had 73.6 mln shares outstanding.  Reuter &#3;
