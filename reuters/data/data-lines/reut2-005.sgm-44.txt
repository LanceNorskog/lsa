TEXAS AIR <TEX> NAMES BRITT AIRWAYS PRESIDENT

Texas Air Corp said it named Norman McInnis as president of its Britt Airways unit, succeeding Bill Britt, who retired March one.
 McInnis, former president of Royale Airlines, most recently was a consultant to the commuter airline industry.   Reuter &#3;
