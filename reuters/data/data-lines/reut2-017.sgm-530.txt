INNOVATIVE SOFTWARE <INSO> TO OFFER CONVERTIBLES

Innovative Software Inc said it plans to sell later this month or in July about 25 mln dlrs of convertible subordinated debentures due 2012.
 Proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes, including possible acquisitions of complementary products, technologies or companies, Innovative Software said.  Reuter &#3;
