INTERNATIONAL MOBILE <IMMC> FILES FOR OFFERING

International Mobile Machines Corp said it filed for a public offering of one mln shares of convertible preferred stock.
 The company said it filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission, and Drexel Burnham Inc and Butcher and Singer Inc will co-manage the offering.  Reuter &#3;
