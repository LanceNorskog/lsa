S-K-I  LTD <SKII> SAYS SKIER VISITS A RECORD

S-K-I Ltd said its skier visits for the curret season have exceeded 1,500,000, up from the record of 1,400,000 set last year.
 The company said its Killington Ski Area in central Vermont is expected to operate into June and its Mount Snow area in southern Vermont will close in May.  Reuter &#3;
