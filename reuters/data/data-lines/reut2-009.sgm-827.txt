NIPPON SIGNAL ISSUES 50 MLN SWISS FRANC NOTES

Nippon Signal Co Ltd is issuing 50 mln Swiss franc of five year notes with warrants, paying an indicated 1-3/8 pct, lead manager Morgan Stanley said.
 Terms will be fixed on April 2 with payment due April 22. The notes are guaranteed by Fuji Bank Ltd.  REUTER &#3;
