CALIFORNIA MICRO DEVICES <CAMD> IN DEFENSE DEAL

California Micro Devices Corp said an additional 3.2 mln dlrs contract was received from General Dynamics Corp <GD> to supply electronic components contained in the guidance control for Defense Electronics Systems.
 This contract follows a 750,000 dlrs contract awarded last year.
 Shipments will begin in APril 1987.
 The company also disclosed that Fuji Photo Film Co Ltd is the licensee of a one mln dlrs technology agreement announced last fall.  Reuter &#3;
