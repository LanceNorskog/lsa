FIRST BANCORP FILES REGISTRATION WITH SEC

First Bancorp said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission its public offering of 400,000  shares of common stock to be underwitten by Interstate Securities Corp.
 First Bancorp said the registration statement also covers 60,000 additional shares that Interstate Securities has the right to acquire in order to cover over allotments.
 First Bancorp, which is now private, said its stock will not be traded until the filing becomes effective.  Reuter &#3;
