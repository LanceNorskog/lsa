NETWORK SECURITY <NTWK.O> HOLDERS TO SELL STOCK

Network Security Corp said three of its major shareholders have executed definitive agreements to sell their shares to Inspectorate International <INSZ.Z> SA.
 The company said its previously-announced merger into Inspectorate is still subject to approval by Network shareholders.  Reuter &#3;
