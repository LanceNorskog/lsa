CHRYSLER <C> EARLY MARCH CAR SALES UP 0.4 PCT

Chrysler Corp said its early March retail sales of domestic-built cars rose 0.4 pct to 25,286 from 25,191 a year ago.
 Chrysler said truck sales in the March 1-10 period rose 15 pct to 15,565 from 13,585 a year ago to the highest level ever for the period.
 There were eight selling days in each period.  Reuter &#3;
