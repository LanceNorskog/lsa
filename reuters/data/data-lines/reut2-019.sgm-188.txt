MELAMINE CHEMICALS FILES FOR INITIAL OFFERING

<Melamine Chemicals Inc> said it has filed for an initial public offering of 2,800,000 common shares through underwriters led by PaineWebber Group Inc <PWJ>.
 The company said it will sell 1,400,000 shares and the rest will be sold by Ashland Oil Inc <ASH> and First Mississippi Corp <FRM>, Melamine's two shareholders.  Proceeds will be used to build a new melamine plant, buy the existing plant from Ashland and First Mississippi and part of a special dividend to Ashland and First Mississippi.
  In Ashland, Ky., Ashland Oil said it is selling 700,000 shares and First Mississippi 700,000 of Melamine.  Reuter &#3;
