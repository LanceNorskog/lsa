E-SYSTEMS <ESY> SEES REDUCED SECOND QUARTER

E-Systems said it expects earnings for the second quarter will likely be below prior expectations due to a reduction in investment income and higher than expected costs on several power amplifier programs.
 The company said it does not expect to match the 52 cts a share net income reported for the second quarter last year.  Reuter &#3;
