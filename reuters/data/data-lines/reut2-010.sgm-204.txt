MOBIL <MOB> POSTPONES HIGHRISE DEVELOPMENT

Mobil Corp said its plans to erect a 72-story office building in downtown Chicago at the site once occupied by a Montgomery Ward department store have been postponed.
 A spokesman for Dearborn Land Co, a unit of Mobil Land Development Corp, said the current office market in the area is overbuilt.  Reuter &#3;
