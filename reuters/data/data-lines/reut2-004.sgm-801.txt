PRIVATE BRANDS INC <PRIBU> TO SEPARATE UNIT

Private Brands Inc said it will separate itself into component parts, all of which will trade individually effective March 16.
 The designer and manufacturer of men's and women's leisure wear said the L.C. Wegard and Co Inc., its underwriter representative, requested the units trade separately.  Reuter &#3;
