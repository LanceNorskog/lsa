NATIONAL FUEL GAS CO <NFG> SETS MAIL DATE

National Fuel Gas Co said its mail date for its previously-announced two-for-one stock split is June 19, 1987.
 The company, which announced the split last week, had said the record date for the split is May 29, 1987.  Reuter &#3;
