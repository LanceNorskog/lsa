PRINCIPAL NEO-TECH SELLS UNIT

<Principal Neo-Tech Inc> said it completed the sale of its subsidiary, Neo-Tech Inc, to Seismic Holdings Inc and Energy Holdings Inc, of Denver, Colo.
 As part of the price, Principal Neo-Tech received notes and preferred shares of Energy Holdings. However, terms were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
