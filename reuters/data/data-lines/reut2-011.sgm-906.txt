MANUFACTURERS HANOVER <MHC> RAISES PRIME RATE

Manufacturers Hanover Trust Co became the third major U.S. bank to increase its prime rate to 7-3/4 pct from 7-1/2, matching a move initiated yesterday by Citibank and Chase Manhattan.
 The bank, the main subsidiary of Manufacturers Hanover Corp, said the new rate is effective today.  Reuter &#3;
