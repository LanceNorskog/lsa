INTERMAGNETICS GENERAL <INMA> COMPLETES BUY

Intermagnetics General Corp said it completed the purchase of the advanced products department of Air Products and Chemicals Inc <APD>.
 Terms were not disclosed.
 The department, which makes cryogenic equipment, will continue operating at its present location in Allentown, Pa., the company said. It will market its products as APD Cryogenics Inc.  Reuter &#3;
