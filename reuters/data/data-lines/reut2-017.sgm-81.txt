HUDSON'S BAY <HBC.TO> SELLS ROXY <CNR.TO> STAKE

Hudson's Bay Co said it agreed to sell its entire 54.5 pct interest in Canadian Roxy Petroleum Ltd, a total of about 7.5 mln shares, to Westcoast Transmission Co Ltd <WTC> for 11 dlrs a share.
 Hudson's Bay said proceeds of about 82 mln dlrs will be used to reduce corporate debt. It said the sale was part of a program of concentrating on its core business of department stores and real estate.
 The sale is subject to regulatory approvals.  Reuter &#3;
