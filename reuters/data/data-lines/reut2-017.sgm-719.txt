KMS INDUSTRIES INC <KMSI.O> GETS CONTRACT

KMS Industries Inc said its Covalent Technology Corp subsidiary won a one-year 60,000 dlr contract for develpoing a rapid and simple method for detecting heaptitis B antigen and diarrheal disease toxins.
 It said the award was from the Program for Appropriate Technology in Health, in collaboration with the United States Agency for International Development.  Reuter &#3;
