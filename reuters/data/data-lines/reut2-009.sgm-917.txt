BLOCKBUSTER <BBEC> TO ACQUIRE LICENSEE

Blockbuster Entertainment Corp said it agreed to buy <Southern Video>, a Blockbuster licensee in San Antonio.
 Blockbuster said it will issue 80,460 shares of its common stock for all the net assets of Southern Video.
 The company said after the acquisition is complete, it intends to open additional Blockbuster Video Superstores in the San Antonio market.  Reuter &#3;
