TOYOTA <TOYOY> HIKES 1987 PRICES

Toyota Motors Sales U.S.A. Inc said it will raise the price of its 1987 cars by an average 1.8 pct, or 208 dlrs, effective immediately.
 Toyota cited the higher yen as the reason for the increase.
 Prices on some models will not be increased, Toyota noted.  Reuter &#3;
