JIFFY LUBE INTERNATIONAL <JLUB> IN AUSTRALIA

Jiffy Lube International Inc said it plans to expand into the Australian market, as well as neighboring countries, with its chain of automotive fast oil change and maintenance centers.
 Jiffy Lube said it plans to open its first center in Brisbane later this year, and have 100 centers open within six years.  Reuter &#3;
