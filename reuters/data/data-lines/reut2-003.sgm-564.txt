CPC <CPC> TO SELL UNIT TO HI-PORT <HIPT>

CPC International Inc said it has agreed in principle to sell its Peterson/Puritain Inc subsidiary to Hi-Port Industries Inc.
 CPC said the sale is not expected to have a significant impact on its earnings and is subject to approval by boards of both companies.  Terms were not disclosed.
 Peterson/Purittan is a contract packager of personal care and household products.  Reuter &#3;
