LONE STAR<LCE> AGREES TO BUY CONCRETE OPERATIONS

Lone Star Industries Inc said it has agreed to acquire ready-mixed concrete and aggregates businesses from <Riedel International Inc> of Portland, Ore., for an undisclosed amount of cash.
 Lone Star's one sentence statement gave no further details and company spokesmen were not available.  Reuter &#3;
