MOBIL <MOB> RAISES PLASTIC GROCERY BAG CAPACITY

Mobil Corp's Mobile Chemical Co said it is expanding its plastic grocery sack production by 20 pct with two new manufacturing lines at its Temple, Texas, plant.
 Mobil said the new lines will begin operation next year.
 A spokesman said no information on cost or capacity of the equipment is available.   Reuter &#3;
