ALMI GROUP ACQUIRES INTEREST IN CLEARVIEW BAKING

<ALMI Group L.P.> said its co-chairmen, Michael Landes and Albert Schwartz, and its vice chairman, Michael Spiegler, have acquired a 50 pct equity interest in <Clearview Baking Corp> for undisclosed terms.
 A. Dale Mayo, Clearview's president and chief executive officer, will retain a 50 pct interest in the company, ALMI Group said.  Reuter &#3;
