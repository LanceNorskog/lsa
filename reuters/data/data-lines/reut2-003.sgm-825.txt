PRIMAGE <PRIM> INTRODUCES NEW PRINTER

Primages Inc said it introduced a new daisywheel printer that can print 90 characters per second and features graphics capabilities.
 The printer, called Primage 90-GT, is priced at 1,500 dlrs and can be combined with the company's paper-handling systems, Primage said.  Reuter &#3;
