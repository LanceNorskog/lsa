FINANCIAL SECURITY <FSSLA> TO SELL BRANCH

Financial Security Savings and Loan Association said it has agreed to sell its Sunrise, Fla., branch to Fortune Financial Group Inc <FORF> of Clearwater, Fla., for a "substantial profit," subject to regulatory approval.
