LOTUS <LOTS>, MCI <MCIC> SHIP SOFTWARE PACKAGE

Lotus Development Corp and MCI Communications Corp said they made the first shipment of Lotus Express for MCI Mail, a communications-software product that enables personal computer users to exchange messages, send telexes and transfer files.
 Lotus Express was announced last December and was shipped on schedule, the companies said.  Reuter &#3;
