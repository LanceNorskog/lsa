AMERITECH <AIT> UNIT NAMES NEW PRESIDENT

Ameritech Publishing, a unit of Ameritech, the midwest-based telephone services company, said it has named Barry Allen president effective April.
 Ameritech Publishing said Allen will succeed Leo Egan, who will become chairman and chief executive officer until he retires on June 1.  Reuter &#3;
