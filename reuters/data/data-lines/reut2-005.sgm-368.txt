CME, CBOE SET JOINT PRESS CONFERENCE FOR TODAY

The Chicago Mercantile Exchange, CME, and the Chicago Board Options Exchange, CME, will hold a joint news conference at 1200 CST (1800 GMT) today.
 Neither exchange would elaborate on the topic of the press conference, although a CBOE spokesperson said it will deal a new financial product.  Reuter &#3;
