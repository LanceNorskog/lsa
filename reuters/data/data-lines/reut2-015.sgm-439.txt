CONTI SHARES OPEN HIGHER ON GOOD NEW TYRE REVIEWS

Shares of Continental Gummi-Werke AG <CONG.F> opened eight marks stronger in an otherwise mixed Frankfurt market and dealers attributed the trend to favourable press reports about a new tyre system the company is developing.
 The shares later eased to 344.50.
 A spokesman for the West German Automobile Association, ADAC, said the ADAC magazine in a recent edition described advantages of the new tyre.  REUTER &#3;
