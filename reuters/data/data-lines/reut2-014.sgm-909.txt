FRENCH FEBRUARY CAR OUTPUT UP 27.2 PCT

February car production totalled 269,745 this year, up 27.2 pct on a year-on-year basis, the Automobile Constructors' Association (CSCA) said.
 This brought total car output in the first two months of the year to 504,266, a 14.1 pct year-on-year rise, it said.  REUTER &#3;
