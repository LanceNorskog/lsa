ASSOCIATES CORP ISSUES 15 BILLION YEN BOND

<Associates Corp of North America> is issuing a 15 billion yen eurobond due November 2, 1992 with a five pct coupon and priced at 102-3/8, <IBJ International Ltd> said as lead manager.
 The non-callable bonds will be issued in denominations of one mln yen and will be listed in Luxembourg. Fees are 1-1/4 pct for selling and 5/8 pct for management and underwriting combined, with a 1/8 pct praecipuum. Pay date is April 6.
 Co-lead managers are <New Japan Securities>, <Wako Securities> and <Yasuda Trust>.  REUTER &#3;
