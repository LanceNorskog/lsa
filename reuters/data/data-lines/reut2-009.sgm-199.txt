SAAB-SCANIA LAUNCHES 100 MLN DLR EURO-CP PROGRAM

Saab-Scania AB is establishing a 100 mln dlr euro-commercial paper issuance program, Enskilda Securities said as arranger.
 Dealers will be Enskilda and Bankers Trust International Ltd. Paper will be issued in discounted note form only and will have maturities between seven and 365 days.
 Denominations will be 500,000 and one mln dlrs.
 Enskilda Securities said Saab-Scania hopes to activate the program soon.  REUTER &#3;
