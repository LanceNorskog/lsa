AUDI OF AMERICA LIFTS CAR PRICES

Audi of America Inc said it will increase prices on all models by an average of 3.9 pct, or 838 dlrs, effective July 20.
 The increase applies to Audi 4000S, 4000CS Quattro, Coupe GT, 5000S sedan and wagon, 5000S Quattro, 5000CS Turbo, and 5000CS Turbo Quattro sedan and wagon models, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
