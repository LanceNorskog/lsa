SPAIN GIVES CUBA 25 MLN DLR CREDIT LINE

Spain has granted Cuba a 25 mln dlr credit line for the purchase of Spanish goods and services in the iron and steel industry, the repair of fishing boats and development of industrial projects, Prensa Latina said.
 The credit line was agreed by a joint economic/industrial commission.
 The commission also discussed the possible participation of spanish companies in the Cuban tourist industry.  REUTER &#3;
