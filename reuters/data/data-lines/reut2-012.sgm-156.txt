BANK OF NOVA SCOTIA TO APPLY FOR TOKYO LISTING

<Bank of Nova Scotia> said it would apply for a common share listing on the Tokyo Stock Exchange.
 The bank, Canada's fourth largest, said it expected its listing to occur later this year. It said Nomura Securities Co Ltd, of Tokyo, would sponsor its application.   Reuter &#3;
