NITTO BOSEKI LAUNCHES 60 MLN SFR 4-7/8 PCT BOND

Nitto Boseki Co Ltd of Tokyo is launching a 60 mln Swiss franc 4-7/8 pct bond due April 27, 1995 at 100-1/4 pct, lead manager Union Bank of Switzerland said.
 Subscriptions close April 8 and payment date is April 27.  REUTER &#3;
