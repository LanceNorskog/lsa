ACTON AUTO RENTAL INITIAL OFFERING STARTS

Lead underwriter McDonald and Co Investments Inc <MDD> said an offering of 900,000 shares of Action Auto Rental Inc is underway, priced at 12 dlrs per share.
 Undewriters have been granted an option to purchase up to another 135,000 shares to cover overallotments.
 The company is selling 697,500 shares and shareholders the rest.  Reuter &#3;
