E.F. HUTTON <EFH> SETS FINANCES TELEPHONE DEAL

E.F. Hutton Group Inc said it is providing financing for the previously announced acquisition of <Virgin Islands Telephone Co> by <Atlantic Tele-Network Co>.
 Terms of the financing were not disclosed.
 Virgin Islands Telephone, which operates telephone systems in the U.S. Virgin Islands, handles about 1.5 mln local calls per week and one mln long-distance calls per month, the company said.   Reuter &#3;
