HOLDER <HOLD.O> REORGANIZATION PLAN APPROVED

Holder Communications Corp said stockholders approved its reorganization plan with the <General Masonry Group> in Nashville, Tenn.
 The company said also approved was its name change to GMX Communications Inc and an increase of authorized shares to 100 mln from 20 mln.
 Included in the reorganization, which is scheduled for closing June 30, was the installation of a new sla e of five directors.
 Holder said it owns eight radio stations and General Masonry is engaged in masonry construction.  Reuter &#3;
