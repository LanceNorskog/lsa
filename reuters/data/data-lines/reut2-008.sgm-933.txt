CANDLEWOOD BANK INITIAL OFFERING STARTS

Underwriter Moseley Holding Corp <MOSE> said an initial public offering of 500,000 common shares of Candlewood Bank and Trust Co is underway at 10 dlrs per share.
 It said it has been granted an option to buy up to 75,000 more shares to cover overallotments.  Directors and officers of the bank are expected to buy about 110,000 common shares at the public offering price, it said.  Reuter &#3;
