FISHER TRANSPORTATION FILES FOR INITIAL OFFERING

<Fisher Transportation Services Inc> said it has filed for an initial public offering of 1,100,000 common shares at an expected price of six to seven dlrs a share through underwriters led by Laidlaw Adams and Peck Inc.
 Fisher is an irregular route truckload quantity general commodity carrier operating throughout the continental U.S.  Reuter &#3;
