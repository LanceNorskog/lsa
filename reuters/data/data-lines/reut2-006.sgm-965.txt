BRITISH LAND CONVERTIBLE BOND RAISED TO 33 MLN STG

British Land Co Plc <BLND.L> convertible eurobond issued yesterday has been raised to a total of 33 mln stg from the 30 mln originally indicated, lead manager Credit Suisse First Boston Ltd said.
 The coupon has been fixed at 7.50 pct and the conversion price at 248 pence to give a 27.84 pct premium over last night's share price close on the London stock exchange.  REUTER &#3;
