AMAX INC <AMX> USES POND SYSTEM FOR MAGNESIUM

AMAX Inc unit, AMAX Magnesium Corp, said it will build a Knolls Solar Evaporation Pond System for recovery of magnesium.
 The company said the system will replace a previous recovery system which was flooded in 1986.
 The company said construction should be complete by 1988 and in full production by 1989.  Reuter &#3;
