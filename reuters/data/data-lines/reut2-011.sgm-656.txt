ALLIED-SIGNAL <ALD> COMPLETES UNIT SALE

Allied-Signal Inc said it completed the previously announced sale of its Linotype Group to Commerzbank AG of West Germany.
 The purchase price was not disclosed.
 Eschborn, West Germany-based Linotype had 1986 sales of more than 200 mln dlrs, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
