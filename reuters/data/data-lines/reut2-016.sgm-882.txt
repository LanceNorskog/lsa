CITIZENS SAVINGS <CSBF.O> NAMES NEW CHAIRMAN

Citizens Savings Bank FSB said chairman and chief executive officer Frank L. Hewitt Jr. is retiring and is being succeeded by vice chairman Clinton C. Sisson.
 The company said Hewitt will remain chairman and president of its First Citizens Corp subsidiary.  Reuter &#3;
