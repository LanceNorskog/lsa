EXECUTIVE TELECOM <XTEL> CHANGES MEETING DATE

Executive Telecommunications Inc said the recently announced joint board of directors meeting with <First Equipment Leasing Co> to negotiate the details of the First Equipment reverse takeover offer has been changed from March 12 to March 16.
 XTEL said the move was made to accommodate several of the directors with conflicting schedules.
 First Equipment recently offered to buy XTEL in a reverse takeover deal in which the new company would retain the XTEL name.  Reuter &#3;
