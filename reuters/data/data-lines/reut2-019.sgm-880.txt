AMERICAN CENTURY <ACT> UNIT SELLS STOCK

American Century Corp said its Commerce Savings unit will sell 997,756 common shares in First American Financial Corp <FAMR.O>, representing 18 pct of First American.
 The company said the shares are being repurchased by First American for 29.4 mln dlrs and two real estate assets in California.
 It said Commerce Savings acquired the shares in 1982 and 1983.  Reuter &#3;
