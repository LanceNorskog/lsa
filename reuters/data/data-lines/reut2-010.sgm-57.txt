FIDELITY <FNF> UNIT ACQUIRES CALIFORNIA CONCERN

Fidelity National Financial Inc said its Fidelity National Title Insurance Co subsidiary acquired the operations of Safeco Title Insurance Co in the northern California counties of Alameda, Contra Costa and San Mateo.
 Terms of the acquisition were not disclosed.  Reuter &#3;
