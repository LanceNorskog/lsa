BESICORP, LCP CHEMICALS IN COGENERATION PACT

<Besicorp Group Inc> said it reached an agreement with <LCP Chemicals and Plastics Inc> to develop an 80 megawatt cogeneration station at LCP's facility in Syracuse N.Y., in a project estimated to require about 50 mln dlrs in financing.
 Under the agreement, it said a standard power sale agreement is expected to be entered into with Niagara Mohawk Power Corp <NMK> for the sale of electrical power output.
 Besicrop said it is developing the project with <Kamine Engineering and Mechanical Contracting <Inc>, and both companies will retain long-term interests in the project.  Reuter &#3;
