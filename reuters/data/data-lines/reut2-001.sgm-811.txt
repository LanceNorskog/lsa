YUGO FEBRUARY CAR SALES BREAK RECORD

<Yugo America Inc>, a unit of <Yugo NV>, said it sold 5,059 cars in February, the first month in which sales topped 5,000.
 Yugo said the February's sales were 21.2 pct higher than sales in January and 191.4 pct above the 1,736 cars sold in the same month last year.
 Yugo said it has sold a total of 9,233 cars so far in 1987.  Reuter &#3;
