RJR NABISCO <RJR> FILES 500 MLN DLR DEBT OFFER

RJR Nabisco Inc filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a shelf offering of up to 500 mln dlrs of debt securities on terms to be set at the time of sale.
 The company said proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes including repurchases of RJR securities.
 Proceeds also may be used for working capital, reduction of short-term debt and capital expenditures, it said.
 Underwriters for the offering were not named in the draft prospectus.  Reuter &#3;
