WINDMERE <WDMR> IN JOINT MERCHANDISING VENTURE

Windmere Corp said it will form a joint venture with <Paragon Sales Inc>, and will be called  Paragon Industries.
 The equally-owned joint venture will merchandise and distribute electric heaters, humidifiers and other electric seasonal consumer products in the U.S., according to the company.
 The two companies, which have approximately 15 pct of the electric fan market in the U.S., will attempt to expand that market and introduce new products, Windmere said.  Reuter &#3;
