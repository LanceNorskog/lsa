DIASONIWS <DNC> SAYS IMAGING SYSTEM APPROVED

Diasonics Inc said the U.S. Food and Drug Administration has given premarket approval to <Instrumentarium Corp> of Finland's ULF magnetic resonance imaging system.
 Diasonics already distributes the system in several countries and will distribute it in the U.S. as well.  Reuter &#3;
