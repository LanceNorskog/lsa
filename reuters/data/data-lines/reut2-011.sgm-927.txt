DIVI HOTELS <DVH> WARRANT PRICE ADJUSTED

Divi Hotels NV of Aruba said the exercise price of its warrants has been cut to 11 dlrs per share from 12 dlrs until September 17 and to 13 dlrs from 14 dlrs thereafter due to the impact of a recent 1,500,000 share offering.  Reuter &#3;
