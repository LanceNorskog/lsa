NEW JERSEY RESOURCES <NJR> TO OFFER SHARES

New Jersey Resources corp said it has filed for an offering of 1,300,000 common shares through underwriters led by Merrill Lynch and Co Inc <MER> and E.F. Hutton Group Inc <EFH>.
 It said the offering is expected to be completed in late March or early April and proceeds will be used to reduce short-term debt.  Reuter &#3;
