BANK OF THAILAND FLOATS NEW GOVERNMENT BOND

The Bank of Thailand said it has floated 500 mln baht of new five-year loan bonds for public sale.
 The bonds will yield 7.25 pct net after tax with interest compounded semi-annually.
 The aggregate five-year yield will amount to 42.77 pct of the principal on maturity.  REUTER &#3;
