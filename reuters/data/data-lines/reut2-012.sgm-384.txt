HUGHES <GMH> ELECTS CHAIRMAN

General Motors Corp's Hughes Aircraft Co said it named Albert Wheelon as chairman and chief executive officer, succeeding Allen Puckett who retired after more than 38 years with the company.
 Wheelon has served in key management positions at Hughes since joining in 1966.  Reuter &#3;
