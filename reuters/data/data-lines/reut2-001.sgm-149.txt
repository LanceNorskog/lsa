RESERVE OIL <ROIL> HOLDERS APPROVE NAME CHANGE

Reserve Oil and Minerals Corp said its shareholders approved changing the name of the company to Reserve Industries Corp.
 The company said it has contracted to purchase, process and recycle various waste materials generated at the Ogden, Utah, zirconium plant of Westinghouse Electric Corp's <WX> Western Zieconium subsidiary.  Reuter &#3;
