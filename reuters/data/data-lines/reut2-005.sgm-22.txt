JWP <JWP> SELLS 30 MLN DLRS OF NOTES

JWP Inc said it sold 30 mln dlrs principal amount of its 9.25 pct senior notes due 1996 to certain institutional lenders.
 The company said about 15 mln dlrs of the proceeds will be used to pay debt and the balance for general corporate purposes.  Reuter &#3;
