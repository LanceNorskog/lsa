COMDATA <CDN> GETS MERGER FINANCING

Comdata Network Inc said it has received a letter from Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc stating that, subject to conditions, the company could raise up to 235 mln dlrs in debt securities to finance the previously announced merger of a newly formed corporation controlled by <Welsh, Carson, Anderson and Stowe IV> and Comdata.
 Comdata said the letter was in accordance with the previously announced terms with Welsh, Carson.  Reuter &#3;
