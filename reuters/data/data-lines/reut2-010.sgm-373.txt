ANDREOTTI DROPS COALITION BID

Veteran politician Giulio Andreotti abandoned his attempts to form a government, putting Italy on course for early elections, political sources said.
 Christian Democrat Andreotti told President Francesco Cossiga he was unable to reconstruct the five-party coalition after two weeks of negotiations, they said.  Reuter &#3;
