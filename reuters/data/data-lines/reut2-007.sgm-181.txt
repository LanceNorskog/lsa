DOMTAR TO ISSUE 100 MLN DLRS OF DEBENTURES

<Domtar Inc> said it filed a preliminary prospectus for an issue of 100 mln dlrs of 10 pct debentures, to be offered at 99-3/4 to yield 10.03 pct and to mature April 15, 2011.
 Underwriters are Levesque, Beaubien Inc, Wood Gundy Inc, Burns Fry Ltd and Nesbitt Thomson Deacon Ltd.  Reuter &#3;
