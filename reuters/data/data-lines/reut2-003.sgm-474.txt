NORSK HYDRO ISSUES 50 MLN STG BONDS

Norsk Hydro A/S <NHY.O> is issuing 50 mln stg bonds due April 15, 1993 carrying a 9-7/8 pct coupon and priced at 101-5/8, said Hambros Bank Ltd as lead manager.
 Norsk Hydro is 51 pct owned by the Kingdom of Norway. The bonds, which are available in denominations of 1,000 and 10,000 stg will be listed on the London Stock Exchange.
 Payment date is April 15. Fees consist of a 1-1/4 pct selling concession and 5/8 pct combined management and underwriting.  REUITER &#3;
