SOUTHWEST BANCORP <SWB> TO EXCHANGE PREFERRED

Southwest Bancorp said it will commence an exchange offer for all of its 108,710 issued and outstanding shares of 2.50-dlr cumulative convertible preferred stock on March 12.
 Southwest said it will issue 11 shares of its no par value common stock in exchange for each preferred share.  Reuter &#3;
