VMS STRATEGIC <VLANS> SETS INITIAL DIVIDEND

VMS Strategic Land Trust said it delcared an initial quarterly cash dividend of 30 cts a share, payable May 15 to shareholders of record April 20.
 The company also said that effective today it will be trading on the NASDAQ system under the symbol <VLANS>.  Reuter &#3;
