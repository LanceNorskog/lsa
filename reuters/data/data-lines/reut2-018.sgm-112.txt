GATEWAY <GWAY.O> CUTS ADAPTER CARD COST 25 PCT

Gateway Communications Inc said it reduced the price of its G/NET network adapter card 25 pct to 395 dlrs from 525 dlrs.
 Gateway said the local area network (LAN) market has become very popular in the computer industry, and said LAN suppliers are finding end users more receptive to and demanding of LAN technology.  Reuter &#3;
