SUMITOMO METAL SETS UP LONDON, OSAKA FINANCE FIRMS

<Sumitomo Metal Industries Ltd> has set up two finance firms, one in Osaka and one in London, a company spokesman said.
 <Sumitomo Metal International Finance Plc> in London is wholly-owned. <Fuso Finance Co Ltd> in Osaka, is owned 50 pct by Sumitomo with the rest equally shared by two small Sumitomo Metal subsidiaries.
 The two new companies will work for the Sumitomo Metal group on fund raising and investment in financial markets.  REUTER &#3;
