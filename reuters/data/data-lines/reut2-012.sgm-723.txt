THAI AIRWAYS BUYS TWO MCDONNELL <MD> DC-10S

Thai Airways International Ltd said it purchased two McDonnell Douglas Corp's DC-10 extended range wide-body jets for over 125 mln dlrs.
 The jets will be delivered at year end.
 Thai Airways said the jets will operate flights between Thailand, northern Europe and New Zealand.  Reuter &#3;
