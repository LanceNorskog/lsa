FRENCH CAISSE NATIONALE DES AUTOROUTES ISSUES BOND

Caisse Nationale des Autoroutes is issuing a three billion French franc bond, the French bond issuing committee said.
 The issue will be lead-managed by Caisse Nationale du Credit Agricole, Credit Lyonnais and Cie Financiere de Paribas subsidiary Credit du Nord.  REUTER &#3;
