DOW <DOW> SETS GROUNDWATER POLLUTION VENTURE

Dow Chemical Co, <Guy F. Atkinson Co> and <Woodward-Clyde Consultants> said they formed a venture to combat groundwater contamination.
 The venture, called AWD Technologies Inc, will offer site analysis, planning, engineering and construction services for restoration of groundwater.  Reuter &#3;
