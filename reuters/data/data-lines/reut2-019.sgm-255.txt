BOEING <BA> MERGER WAITING PERIOD EXPIRES

Boeing Co said the Hart-Scott-Rodino waiting period required in connection with its pending tender offer for ArgoSystems Inc <ARGI.O> expired at midnight June 18.
 Boeing began its 37 dlr per share cash tender offer for the defense electronics firm on June two.  Reuter &#3;
