AC TELECONNECT <ACCC> ADDS CANADA PHONE SERVICE

AC Teleconnect Corp's unit, ACC Long Distance Corp, said customers in Binghamton, Buffalo, Rochester and Syracuse, N.Y., will be able to place calls to Canada.
 The company also said it plans to offer overseas telephone service to a number of countries in the summer 1987.  Reuter &#3;
