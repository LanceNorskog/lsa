MCCHIP <MCS.TO> TO ACQUIRE RESERVE HOLDING

McChip Resources Inc said it has agreed to exchange its interest in Oklahoma oil and gas properties operated by Reserve Exploration Co <REXC.O> for 638,435 restricted Reserve common shares.
 The company said it will have a 44 pct stake in Reserve's outstanding shares as a result of the exchange.  Reuter &#3;
