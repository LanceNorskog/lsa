INT'L GAME TECHNOLOGY <IGAM> TO SELL DEBT

International Game Technology said it filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission a registration statement covering a 35 mln dlr issue of senior notes due 1995 and a 25 mln dlr issue of convertible senior subordinated debentures due 2002.
 The company named Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc as sole manager of the offerings.  Reuter &#3;
