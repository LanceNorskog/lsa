GENERAL ELECTRIC <GE> SELLS ENGINES TO BAHRAIN

General Electric Co said the Bahrain Amiri Air Force selected its engines to power 12 of its General Dynamics Corp <GD> fighter aircraft.
 The company did not disclose the price of the engines.
 The company said Bahrain was the fourth foreign nation to select the engine. The others are the Greek, Israeli and Turkish air forces, the company said.  Reuter &#3;
