E-TRON NAMES DIRECTOR OF CHAFF DIVISION

<E-tron> said it named Thomas Fleming director of its Chaff Countermeasures Division.
 Fleming, who has 18 years experience in Chaff manufacturing, was formerly with Tracor Inc <TRR>, the company said.   E-Tron said it is currently in Chapter 11.  Reuter &#3;
