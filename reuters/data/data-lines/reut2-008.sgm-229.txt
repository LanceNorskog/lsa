BLOCKBUSTER <BBEC> UNIT SIGNS EXCLUSIVE LICENSE

Blockbuster Entertainment Corp said its Blockbuster Distribution Corp unit granted an exclusive license agreement to <Video Superstore Management Inc>.
 Blockbuster said the license gives Video the right to own and operate its Blockbuster Videos Rental Superstores for the Milwaukee, Wis., area.  Reuter &#3;
