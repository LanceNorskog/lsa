FOOTHILL <FGI> TO TRADE ON NYSE

Foothill Group Inc said its stock began trading Tuesday on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol "FGI."
 Foothill's shares had been traded on the American Stock Exchange.
 Foothill is an independent commercial finance company which operates through two subsidiaries -- Foothill Capital Copr and Foothill Thrift and Loan.  Reuter &#3;
