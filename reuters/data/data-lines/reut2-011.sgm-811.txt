U.K. MERGER CLEARANCES

The Secretary of State for Trade and Industry said he had decided not to refer the proposed acquisition by Reed International Plc <REED.L> of <Technical Publishing Company Inc> to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission.
 The proposed acquisition by <Rosehaugh Plc> of <The General Funds Investment Trust Plc> was also cleared.  REUTER &#3;
