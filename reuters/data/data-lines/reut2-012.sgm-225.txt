INSPEECH <INSP> BUYS NORMA BORK, BORK MEDICAL

Inspeech Inc said it acquired Norma Bork Associates Inc and Bork Medical Services Inc for undisclosed terms.
 These firms, with combined revenues of about one mln dlrs, are providers of speech pathology, physical therapy and occupational therapy services.  Reuter &#3;
