BLACK HAWK MINING CAN'T EXPLAIN STOCK RISE

<Black Hawk Mining Inc> said it knows of "no major activities" to account for the recent rise in the company's stock price.
 The shares rose 11 cts to 88 cts on the Toronto Stock Exchange yesterday.
 The company added that its application for its proposed acquisition of <Platinova Resources Ltd> and financings are still before the listing committee of the stock exchange.  Reuter &#3;
