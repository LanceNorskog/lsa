PECHINEY TO SELL PART OF STAKE IN CANADA PLANT

French state-owned aluminium and special metals group Pechiney <PUKG.PA> is planning to sell 25 pct of a Canadian aluminium plant at Becancour to the U.S.'s Reynolds Metals Co <RLM.N> over the coming weeks, the company said.
  Pechiney currently holds a majority 50.1 pct stake in the plant. The company also said it planned an agreement soon with Italy's <La Metalli Industriale>, a 57 pct owned subsidiary of <Societa Metallurgica Italiana> related to cupreous products. No further details were available.  REUTER &#3;
