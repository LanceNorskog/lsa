BROCKWAY AIR TO BUY FIVE SAAB PLANES

The U.S. Airline Brockway Air, a subsidiary of <Brockway Inc>, has signed an agreement with Saab-Scania AB <SABS ST> to buy five 34-seater Saab SF340 aircraft for an undisclosed price, with an option on a further five, Saab said.
  All five will be delivered to the Burlington, Vermont-based airline this year for use on Brockway's 22-city service system.
 Saab said it now had 103 firm orders for the regional SF340, 80 of which had already been delivered.  REUTER &#3;
