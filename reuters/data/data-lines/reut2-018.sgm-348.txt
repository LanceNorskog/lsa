ALLERGY GETS GRANT TO DEVELOP INTERLEUKIN KIT

Allergy Immuno Technologies Inc said it received federal funding to develop a diagnostic kit for measuring Interleukin-2 and Interleukin-2 receptor.
 Interleukin is a naturally produced substance important in maintaining the body's immune system.  Reuter &#3;
